(to_angle)¶




Parameters:to_angle – a number (integer or float)



Set the orientation of the turtle to to_angle.  Here are some common
directions in degrees:






standard mode
logo mode



0 - east
0 - north

90 - north
90 - east

180 - west
180 - south

270 - south
270 - west
