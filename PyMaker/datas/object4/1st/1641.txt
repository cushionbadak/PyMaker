compare(other, context=None)Â¶
Compare the values of two Decimal instances.  compare() returns a
Decimal instance, and if either operand is a NaN then the result is a
NaN:
a or b is a NaN  ==> Decimal('NaN')
a < b            ==> Decimal('-1')
a == b           ==> Decimal('0')
a > b            ==> Decimal('1')
