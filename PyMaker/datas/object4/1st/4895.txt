<code class="descclassname">ReadTransport.</code><code class="descname">resume_reading</code><span class="sig-paren">(</span><span class="sig-paren">)</span><a class="headerlink" href="#asyncio.ReadTransport.resume_reading" title="Permalink to this definition">¶</a></dt>
<dd><p>Resume the receiving end.  The protocol’s
<a class="reference internal" href="#asyncio.Protocol.data_received" title="asyncio.Protocol.data_received"><code class="xref py py-meth docutils literal notranslate"><span class="pre">protocol.data_received()</span></code></a> method
will be called once again if some data is available for reading.</p>
<div class="versionchanged">
<p><span class="versionmodified">Changed in version 3.7: </span>The method is idempotent, i.e. it can be called when the
transport is already reading.</p>
</div>
</dd>
