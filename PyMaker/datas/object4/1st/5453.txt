<code class="descclassname">signal.</code><code class="descname">sigwait</code><span class="sig-paren">(</span><em>sigset</em><span class="sig-paren">)</span><a class="headerlink" href="#signal.sigwait" title="Permalink to this definition">Â¶</a></dt>
<dd><p>Suspend execution of the calling thread until the delivery of one of the
signals specified in the signal set <em>sigset</em>.  The function accepts the signal
(removes it from the pending list of signals), and returns the signal number.</p>
<p class="availability"><a class="reference internal" href="intro.html#availability"><span class="std std-ref">Availability</span></a>: Unix (see the man page <em class="manpage">sigwait(3)</em> for further
information).</p>
