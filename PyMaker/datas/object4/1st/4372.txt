starmap(func, iterable[, chunksize])Â¶
Like map() except that the elements of the iterable are expected
to be iterables that are unpacked as arguments.
Hence an iterable of [(1,2), (3, 4)] results in [func(1,2),
func(3,4)].

New in version 3.3.
