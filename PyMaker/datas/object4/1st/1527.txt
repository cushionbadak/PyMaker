To Complex, Real adds the operations that work on real
numbers.
In short, those are: a conversion to float, math.trunc(),
round(), math.floor(), math.ceil(), divmod(), //,
%, <, <=, >, and >=.
Real also provides defaults for complex(), real,
imag, and conjugate().
