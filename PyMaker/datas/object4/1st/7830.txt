turtle.home()¶
Move turtle to the origin – coordinates (0,0) – and set its heading to
its start-orientation (which depends on the mode, see mode()).
>>> turtle.heading()
90.0
>>> turtle.position()
(0.00,-10.00)
>>> turtle.home()
>>> turtle.position()
(0.00,0.00)
>>> turtle.heading()
0.0
