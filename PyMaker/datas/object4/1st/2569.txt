The time and date of the last modification to the archive member.  This is a
tuple of six values:






Index
Value



0
Year (>= 1980)

1
Month (one-based)

2
Day of month (one-based)

3
Hours (zero-based)

4
Minutes (zero-based)

5
Seconds (zero-based)




Note
The ZIP file format does not support timestamps before 1980.
