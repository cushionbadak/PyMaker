turtle.bgcolor(*args)¶




Parameters:args – a color string or three numbers in the range 0..colormode or a
3-tuple of such numbers



Set or return background color of the TurtleScreen.
>>> screen.bgcolor("orange")
>>> screen.bgcolor()
'orange'
>>> screen.bgcolor("#800080")
>>> screen.bgcolor()
(128.0, 0.0, 128.0)
