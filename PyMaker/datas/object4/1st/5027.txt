<code class="descclassname">socket.</code><code class="descname">CAN_RAW_FD_FRAMES</code><a class="headerlink" href="#socket.CAN_RAW_FD_FRAMES" title="Permalink to this definition">Â¶</a></dt>
<dd><p>Enables CAN FD support in a CAN_RAW socket. This is disabled by default.
This allows your application to send both CAN and CAN FD frames; however,
you one must accept both CAN and CAN FD frames when reading from the socket.</p>
<p>This constant is documented in the Linux documentation.</p>
<p class="availability"><a class="reference internal" href="intro.html#availability"><span class="std std-ref">Availability</span></a>: Linux &gt;= 3.6.</p>
