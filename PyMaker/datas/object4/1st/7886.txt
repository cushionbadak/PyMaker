turtle.tilt(angle)¶




Parameters:angle – a number



Rotate the turtleshape by angle from its current tilt-angle, but do not
change the turtle’s heading (direction of movement).
>>> turtle.reset()
>>> turtle.shape("circle")
>>> turtle.shapesize(5,2)
>>> turtle.tilt(30)
>>> turtle.fd(50)
>>> turtle.tilt(30)
>>> turtle.fd(50)
