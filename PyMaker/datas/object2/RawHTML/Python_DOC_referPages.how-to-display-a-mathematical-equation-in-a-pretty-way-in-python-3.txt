<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>It is possible to use the <code>%%latex</code> magic function in a Jupyter notebook code cell. This renders the whole cell output in Latex:</p>
<pre><code>%%latex
\begin{align} 2 \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -2 \\ \end{bmatrix} -1 \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ -3 \\ \end{bmatrix} +1 \cdot \begin{bmatrix} -3 \\ 1 \\ \end{bmatrix} -1 \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ \end{bmatrix}= \begin{bmatrix} -4 \\ 1 \\ \end{bmatrix} \end{align}
</code></pre>
<p><a href="https://i.stack.imgur.com/h81J7.png" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><img alt="enter image description here" src="https://i.stack.imgur.com/h81J7.png"/></a></p>
<p>All different methods for rendering LaTeX in Jupyter are described <a href="https://nbviewer.jupyter.org/github/ipython/ipython/blob/2.x/examples/Notebook/Display%20System.ipynb#LaTeX" rel="nofollow noreferrer">here</a>.</p>
