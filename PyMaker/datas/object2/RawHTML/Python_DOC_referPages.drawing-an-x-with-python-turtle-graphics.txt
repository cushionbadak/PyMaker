<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Your first line starts at <code>(0,0)</code> and got to <code>(50, -50)</code>, thus goes 50 units in <strong>X</strong> and <strong>Y</strong> direction. But you second ist going from <code>(0,-50)</code> to <code>(50,50)</code>, thus goes 50 units in <strong>X</strong> direction and <strong>100 units</strong> in <strong>Y</strong> direction. Additionally your right and left have no effect.</p>
<p>One possible solution would be:</p>
<pre><code>
t.setposition(-50,50)
t.pendown()
t.setposition(50,-50)
t.penup()
t.setposition(-50,-50)
t.pendown()
t.setposition(50,50)

</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Another approach you can take is to avoid <code>setposition()</code> altogether and think like a turtle.  That is, crawl forward, backward and turn rather than teleport:</p>
<pre><code>import turtle as t

t.right(45)
t.forward(70)
t.backward(140)
t.forward(70)
t.left(90)
t.forward(70)
t.backward(140)

t.hideturtle()
t.done()
</code></pre>
