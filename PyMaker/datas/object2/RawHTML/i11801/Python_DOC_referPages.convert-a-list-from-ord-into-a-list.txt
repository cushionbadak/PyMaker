<p>I would like to convert what is given from <code>bin</code> into a list.</p>
<p>What I have:</p>
<pre><code>x = input()
for y in x:
    z = (bin(ord(y)))
    print (z)
</code></pre>
<p>What I get:</p>
<pre class="lang-none prettyprint-override"><code>0b1101000
0b1100101
0b1101100
0b1101100
0b1101111
</code></pre>
<p>What I want:</p>
<pre class="lang-none prettyprint-override"><code>[0b1101000, 0b1100101, 0b1101100, 0b1101100, 0b1101111]
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Use a <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/tutorial/datastructures.html#list-comprehensions" rel="nofollow">list comprehension</a>:</p>
<pre><code>z = [bin(ord(y)) for y in x]
print(z)
</code></pre>
<p>That is a shortcut for this:</p>
<pre><code>z = []
for y in x:
    z.append(bin(ord(y))]
print(z)
</code></pre>
</div>
