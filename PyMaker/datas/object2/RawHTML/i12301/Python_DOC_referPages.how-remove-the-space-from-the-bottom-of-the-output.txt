<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Why not rewrite your <code>while</code> loops with <code>for</code> loops which is as I think more appropriate for that task with <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/functions.html#range" rel="nofollow"><code>range</code></a>?</p>
<pre><code>n = 5
for i in range(1, n):
    print('*' * i)
for j in range(n, 0, -1):
    print('*' * j)
</code></pre>
<p>Resulted to:</p>
<pre><code>*
**
***
****
*****
****
***
**
*
</code></pre>
<p><strong>EDIT</strong></p>
<p>Or you could even write it with one liner with two <code>list comprehension</code> and str <code>join</code> method:</p>
<pre><code>n = 3
print('\n'.join(['*' * i for i in range(1, n)] + ['*' * j for j in range(n, 0, -1)]))

*   
**  
*** 
**  
*   
</code></pre>
