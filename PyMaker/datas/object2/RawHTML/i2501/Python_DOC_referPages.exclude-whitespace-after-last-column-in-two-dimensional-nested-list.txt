<p>Use <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/stdtypes.html#str.join" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>str.join()</code></a> to build that string like:</p>
<h3>Code:</h3>
<pre><code>' | '.join(str(x) for x in row)
</code></pre>
<h3>Test Code:</h3>
<pre><code>mult_table = [
    [1, 2, 3],
    [2, 4, 6],
    [3, 6, 9]
]

for row in mult_table:
    print(' | '.join(str(x) for x in row))
</code></pre>
<h3>Results:</h3>
<pre><code>1 | 2 | 3
2 | 4 | 6
3 | 6 | 9
</code></pre>
