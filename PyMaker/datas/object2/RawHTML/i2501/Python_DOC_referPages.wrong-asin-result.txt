<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You're converting the wrong number with the wrong function.</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; import math
&gt;&gt;&gt; x = 0.7071067811865475
&gt;&gt;&gt; math.degrees(math.asin(x))
44.99999999999999
&gt;&gt;&gt;
</code></pre>
<p>That is, given <code>x</code> (which is the sine of an angle) call <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/math.html#math.asin" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>asin</code></a> to compute the angle (in radians), and then use <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/math.html#math.degrees" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>degrees</code></a> to convert that angle to degrees.</p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p><code>math.radians</code> converts <strong>to</strong> radians, you want <code>math.degrees</code>.</p>
<p>It's also in the wrong place, you're converting a number, not an angle. You want </p>
<pre><code>print(math.degrees(math.asin(float(x))))
</code></pre>
<p><a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/math.html#angular-conversion" rel="nofollow noreferrer">https://docs.python.org/3/library/math.html#angular-conversion</a></p>
