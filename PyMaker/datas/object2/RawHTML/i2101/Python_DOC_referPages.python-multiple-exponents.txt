<p><a href="https://docs.python.org/3/reference/expressions.html#operator-precedence" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Exponentiation groups from the right to the left.</a></p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; 2**3**2**1
512
&gt;&gt;&gt; 2**(3**(2**1))
512
</code></pre>
