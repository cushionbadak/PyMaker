<p>If you simply want to remove all non-ASCII characters from <code>string</code>:</p>
<pre><code>print(''.join(c for c in string if ord(c) &lt; 128))
</code></pre>
<p>This outputs:</p>
<pre><code>Im celebrating my sixth month anniversary of no longer being a customer of Star Wars. Ive saved a lot of money
</code></pre>
<p>However, it's better that you find out what the encoding of your original string is and use <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/stdtypes.html#bytes.decode" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>bytes.decode</code></a> to convert it to its original content with nothing removed.</p>
</div>
