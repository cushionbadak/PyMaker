<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>To reach <code>print 'Hi3'</code> you need to go past a <code>return self.start</code> statement. Remember that the <code>return</code> statement exits the function immediately. (Maybe you were mixing up iterators and generators and thought about <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/reference/simple_stmts.html#the-yield-statement" rel="nofollow">yield</a> instead of <code>return</code>).</p>
