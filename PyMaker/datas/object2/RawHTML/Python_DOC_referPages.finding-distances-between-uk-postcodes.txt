<p><a href="https://postcodes.io/" rel="nofollow noreferrer">https://postcodes.io/</a> - service for getting various postcode info. You can use existing instance or you can spin up your own, it's free.</p>
<p>Service above gives you latitude and longitude for the specified postcode. Having two postcodes gives you two points and their lat and long.</p>
<p>Having coordinates of two points makes it possible to define distance between these points. ( be aware that this formula ignores obstacles between points e.g. mountains. ) </p>
<p><a href="https://www.movable-type.co.uk/scripts/latlong.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer">distance by coordinates</a> -  here is the formula.</p>
<p>For more precise distance you can use google API. It's not free.</p>
<p><a href="https://developers.google.com/maps/documentation/distance-matrix/intro" rel="nofollow noreferrer">API</a> - google distance API.</p>
