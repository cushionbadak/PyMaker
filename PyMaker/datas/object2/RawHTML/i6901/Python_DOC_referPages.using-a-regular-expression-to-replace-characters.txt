<p>I want to remove all characters except <code>a-z</code>, <code>A-Z</code>, and spaces from a title. I then want to convert all the characters to lower case.</p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Use the built-in <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/re.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>re</code></a> module.</p>
<pre><code>re.sub(r'[^a-zA-Z ]', '', my_string).lower()
</code></pre>
<p>Here's an example:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; re.sub(r'[^a-zA-Z ]', '', 'This is 1 test string; I like it.').lower()
'this is  test string i like it'
</code></pre>
