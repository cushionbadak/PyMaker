<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Method setTextColor has following prototype (from QTextEdit.py):   </p>
<pre><code>def setTextColor(self, Union, QColor=None, Qt_GlobalColor=None, QGradient=None):
    """ setTextColor(self, Union[QColor, Qt.GlobalColor, QGradient]) """
    pass
</code></pre>
<p>Union here is <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/typing.html#typing.Union" rel="nofollow noreferrer">typing.Union</a>, that used for type hints. Union[QColor, Qt.GlobalColor, QGradient] means "parameter with type QColor or Qt.GlobalColor or QGradient". For some reason it appears in params list too while docstring <code>setTextColor(self, Union[QColor, Qt.GlobalColor, QGradient])</code> is correct. In your program use this method like</p>
<pre><code>self.content_text.setTextColor(QtGui.QColor(124, 124, 134))
</code></pre>
