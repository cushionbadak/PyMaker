<p>In python3 you can use the <a href="https://docs.python.org/2/library/string.html#string.Formatter.format" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>format</code></a> method from string:</p>
<pre><code>print("{:^10.2f}|{:^10.2f}|{:^10.2f}|{:^10.2f}|".format(x,sin(x),cos(x),tan(x)))
</code></pre>
<p>Notice that <code>^</code> means to center, <code>10</code> is the total size of the 'cell' and <code>.2f</code> is to print a float with 2 decimal places, change it according to your needs.</p>
