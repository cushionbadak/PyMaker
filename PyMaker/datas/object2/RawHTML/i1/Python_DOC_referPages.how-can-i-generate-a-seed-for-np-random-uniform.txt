<p>I am trying to create 50 different cities with their latitudes and longitudes however each time I run I want the coordinates to be same.</p>
<pre><code>lat = np.random.uniform(low=-90, high=90, size=50)
long = np.random.uniform(low=-180, high=180, size=50)
</code></pre>
<p>How can I provide a seed?</p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You can do as follow:</p>
<pre><code>np.random.seed(42)
lat = np.random.uniform(low=-90, high=90, size=50)
long = np.random.uniform(low=-180, high=180, size=50)
</code></pre>
