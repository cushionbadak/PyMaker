<p>according to <a href="https://pandas.pydata.org/pandas-docs/stable/reference/api/pandas.read_csv.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer">https://pandas.pydata.org/pandas-docs/stable/reference/api/pandas.read_csv.html</a> , you can add encoding parameter when read the csv file. I suggest you to add "utf-8" or "ISO-8859-1".
pandas.read_csv(yourfile, encoding="utf-8") or pandas.read_csv(yourfile, encoding="ISO-8859-1")</p>
