<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Use <code>map</code> with <code>join</code>:</p>
<pre><code>Data.columns = Data.columns.map('|'.join)
</code></pre>
<p>Or list comprehension:</p>
<pre><code>Data.columns = [f'{a}{b}' for a, b in Data.columns]
</code></pre>
