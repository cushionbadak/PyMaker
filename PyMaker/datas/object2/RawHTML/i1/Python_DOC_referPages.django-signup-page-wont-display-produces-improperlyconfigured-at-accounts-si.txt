<p>Turned out to be a typo. In my view I had <code>from_class</code> rather than <strong><code>form_class</code></strong>.</p>
<p>This is how it should be:</p>
<pre><code>class SignUpView(generic.CreateView):
    form_class = UserCreationForm
    success_url = reverse_lazy('login')
    template_name = 'signup.html'
</code></pre>
