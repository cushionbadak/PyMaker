<p>I would like to determine the sign of the difference between two timestamps in the  format <code>Y-m-d H:M:S</code> contained in Pandas dataframe columns.</p>
<p><code>dtype</code> says these are "M8[ns]" and using:</p>
<pre><code>sign = numpy.sign(df['time1'] - df['time2'])
</code></pre>
<p>returns results like:</p>
<pre><code>-1 days +23:59:59.999999
</code></pre>
<p>While I want a simple -1, 0, +1 as <code>numpy.sign</code> is supposed to do. Can somebody help me to achieve my goal?</p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Convert timedeltas to integers:</p>
<pre><code>sign = numpy.sign((df['time1'] - df['time2']).values.astype(np.int64))
</code></pre>
<p>Or:</p>
<pre><code>sign = numpy.sign((df['time1'] - df['time2']).dt.total_seconds())
</code></pre>
</div>
