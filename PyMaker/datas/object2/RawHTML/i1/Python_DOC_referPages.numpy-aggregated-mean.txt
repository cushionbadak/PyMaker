<p>I want to calculate the mean over a numpy array but within a window from the beginning of the array until the actual value of the array. HereÂ´s an example:</p>
<pre><code>array = [1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, ...]
windowed_means = [1, 1.5, 2, 2.5, ...]
</code></pre>
<p>The calculus are like:</p>
<pre><code>windowed_means = [1/1, (1+2)/2, (1+2+3)/3, (1+2+3+4)/4 , ...]
</code></pre>
<p>Is there any function for that?</p>

<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You can try:</p>
<pre><code>windowed_means = np.cumsum(array)/np.arange(1,len(array)+1)
</code></pre>
<p><code>numpy.cumsum</code> is the <a href="https://docs.scipy.org/doc/numpy/reference/generated/numpy.cumsum.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer">cumulative sum</a>.</p>
