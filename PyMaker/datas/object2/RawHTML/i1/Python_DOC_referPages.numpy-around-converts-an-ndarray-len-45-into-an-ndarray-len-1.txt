<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Change</p>
<p><code>np.around([y_pred_test], decimals=0, out=y_pred_test_round[:,])</code></p>
<p>to</p>
<p><code>np.around(y_pred_test, decimals=0, out=y_pred_test_round[:,])</code></p>
<p>You don't need to put <code>y_pred_test</code> into <code>[]</code> in your case.</p>
