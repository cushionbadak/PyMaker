<p>I have the below table:</p>
<pre><code>+-----------+------+-------+
| member_id | year | count |
+-----------+------+-------+
|         1 | 2012 |     1 |
|         1 | 2013 |     0 |
|         1 | 2014 |     1 |
|         2 | 2012 |     2 |
|         2 | 2013 |     0 |
|         2 | 2014 |     1 |
+-----------+------+-------+
</code></pre>
<p>and i'd like to turn it into this:</p>
<pre><code>+-----------+------------+------------+------------+
| member_id | count_2012 | count_2013 | count_2014 |
+-----------+------------+------------+------------+
|         1 |          1 |          0 |          1 |
|         2 |          2 |          0 |          1 |
+-----------+------------+------------+------------+
</code></pre>
<p>I tried grouping and then pivoting and also just pivoting, but the pivot requires and aggfunc, and I'm not trying to change the values, just reshape it.</p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<pre><code>df = df.pivot(index='member_id',columns='year', values='count')
</code></pre>
<p>If you dont want to have member_id as index:</p>
<pre><code>df = df.pivot(index='member_id',columns='year', values='count').reset_index()
</code></pre>
</div>
