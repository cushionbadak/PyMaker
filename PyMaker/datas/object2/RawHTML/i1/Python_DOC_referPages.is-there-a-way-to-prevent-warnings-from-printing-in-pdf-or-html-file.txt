<p>I am working with JupyterLab and while working with square roots or complex values, python gives <code>RuntimeWarning</code> or <code>ComplexWarning</code>. Is there a way to prevent these warning from showing up when saving the file into a pdf or html?</p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>At the top your script:</p>
<pre><code>import warnings
warnings.filterwarnings("ignore")
</code></pre>
<p><a href="https://docs.python.org/2/library/warnings.html#temporarily-suppressing-warnings" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Temporarily Suppressing Warnings</a></p>
</div>
<span class="comment-copy"><code>python -W ignore foo.py</code> ?</span>
<span class="comment-copy">This works thanks!</span>
