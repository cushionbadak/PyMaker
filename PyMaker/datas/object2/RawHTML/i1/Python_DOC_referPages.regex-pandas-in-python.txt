<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Use <code>pd.Series.str.contains</code> with a regex:</p>
<pre><code>df['new'] = df['data'].str.lower().str.contains('risque de fraude|risques de fraude|risques de frodes|fraud risk|fraude|frode|fraudes|frodes').astype(int)
</code></pre>
<p>And now:</p>
<pre><code>print(df)
</code></pre>
<p>Would result in a dataframe with an additional column, <code>'new'</code> having the expected <code>0</code> and <code>1</code> integer values.</p>
