<p>In <code>R</code> you could do something like</p>
<pre><code>library(data.table)
dt &lt;- data.table(sample(0:2,100,replace=TRUE))
dt[,.N/nrow(dt),V1]

## &gt; dt[,.N/nrow(dt),V1]

##    V1   V1
## 1:  1 0.33
## 2:  2 0.32
## 3:  0 0.35
</code></pre>

<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>without using pandas you could use Counter</p>
<pre><code>from collections import Counter
z = [0,1,1,2]
Counter(z)
Counter({1: 2, 0: 1, 2: 1})
</code></pre>
<p>and then to a dataframe </p>
<pre><code>x = Counter(z)
df = pd.DataFrame.from_dict(x, orient='index').reset_index()
</code></pre>
<p>and then take the values divided by 4 (your desired Freq)</p>
</div>
