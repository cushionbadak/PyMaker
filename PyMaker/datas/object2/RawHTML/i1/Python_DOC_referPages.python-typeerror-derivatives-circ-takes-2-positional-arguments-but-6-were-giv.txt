<p>should maybe be: </p>
<pre><code>afgeleiden = derivatives_circ([theta[n], omega[n], x[n], vx[n], y[n], vy[n]], t[n])
</code></pre>
<p>based on your earlier calls to <code>derivatives_circ</code></p>

<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>In this line:</p>
<pre><code>def derivatives_circ(state, t):
</code></pre>
<p>You defined this function so that it takes <strong>2 arguments</strong>, but then in this line:</p>
<pre><code>afgeleiden = derivatives_circ([theta[n], omega[n]], x[n], vx[n], y[n], vy[n], t[n])
</code></pre>
<p>You are giving it <strong>6 arguments</strong>! That's why it's giving you the error. I don't know the mentality behind your code, but replacing it with this line: </p>
<pre><code>afgeleiden = derivatives_circ([theta[n], omega[n], x[n], vx[n], y[n], vy[n]], t[n])
</code></pre>
