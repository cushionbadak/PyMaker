<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Using <code>pandas</code> plotting functionality:</p>
<pre><code>train_df.plot(x='Pclass', y='Survived', kind='bar')
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>With pure <code>pyplot</code> (<code>import matplotlib.pyplot as plt</code>):</p>
<pre><code>plt.bar(x='Pclass', height='Survived', data=train_df)
</code></pre>
