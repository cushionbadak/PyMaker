<p>I have a pandas DataFrame and I want to reshape it. </p>
<pre><code>  Country       1991  1992  1993  
Deutschland      1     2     3
Griechenland     4     7     0
</code></pre>
<p>How to make a new DataFrame that looks like this:</p>
<pre><code>      Country       Year   Value
    Deutschland     1991     1
    Deutschland     1992     2
    Deutschland     1993     3
    Griechenland    1991     4
    Griechenland    1992     7
    Griechenland    1993     0
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Using <code>melt</code> (and then sorting the values by country and year and resetting the index):</p>
<pre><code>(df.melt('Country', var_name='Year', value_name='Value')
   .sort_values(['Country', 'Year'])
   .reset_index(drop=True))
</code></pre>
<p>Output:</p>
<pre><code>        Country  Year  Value
0   Deutschland  1991      1
1   Deutschland  1992      2
2   Deutschland  1993      3
3  Griechenland  1991      4
4  Griechenland  1992      7
5  Griechenland  1993      0
</code></pre>
</div>
