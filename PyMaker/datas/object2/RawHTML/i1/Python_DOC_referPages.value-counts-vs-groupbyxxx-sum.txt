<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p><code>value_counts()</code> is, count values without looking at what the value actually is. So 1 and 100 on that column will be treated as the same -- you have one count.</p>
<p><code>sum()</code>, however, sum up the columns. So if you have two rows, one is 1 and another is 100, your sum is 101 while the count is 2.</p>
</div>
<span class="comment-copy">chipo.groupby('choice_description')['choice_description'].count() will give your same result</span>
