<p>I have a string:</p>
<pre><code>s = "(1,2)"
</code></pre>
<p>How do I turn it into a tuple?
I can't cast it into a tuple, because:</p>
<pre><code>tuple(s)
</code></pre>
<p>turns s into:</p>
<pre><code>('(', '1', ',', '2', ')')
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Not that fancy but does the job:</p>
<pre><code>s = "(1,2)"    
print(tuple(map(int, s.strip("()").split(","))))
</code></pre>
<p><strong>OUTPUT</strong>:</p>
<pre><code>(1, 2)
</code></pre>
