</code></pre>

<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You pass <code>perm=[1,2,0]</code> to <code>tf.transpose</code>, that's why you get <code>(2, 4, 3)</code> as shape from <code>(3, 2, 4)</code>. (see: <a href="https://www.tensorflow.org/api_docs/python/tf/transpose" rel="nofollow noreferrer">https://www.tensorflow.org/api_docs/python/tf/transpose</a>)</p>
<p>Try <code>X_tensor = tf.transpose(X_1,[2,0,1])</code> to get your desired result.</p>
</div>
