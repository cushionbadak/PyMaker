<p>I have df and list of value and I want to create new df with only value from list. What should I do? </p>
<pre><code>df = pd.DataFrame({'A' : [5,6,3,4,6], 'B' : [1,2,3, 5,1]}) 
list_of_values = [3,6] 
result= df.query("A in @list_of_values")
print(result)
</code></pre>
<p>This is what I have:</p>
<pre><code>   A  B
1  6  2
2  3  3
4  6  1
</code></pre>
<p>This is what I want:</p>
<pre><code>   A  B
0  6  2
1  3  3
2  6  1
</code></pre>

<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Just do a <code>drop=True</code> under <code>reset_index()</code>:</p>
<pre><code>result= df.query("A in @list_of_values").reset_index(drop=True)
</code></pre>
