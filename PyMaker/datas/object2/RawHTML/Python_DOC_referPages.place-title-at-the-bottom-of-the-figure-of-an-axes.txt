<p>You want the title at a specified distance in absolute coordinates from the xaxis. Hence you first position it at <code>y=0</code>. Then you can use some padding in units of points to shift it from there.</p>
<pre><code>ax.set_title("Hello Title", y=0, pad=-25, verticalalignment="top")
</code></pre>
<p>You can use <code>constrained_layout=True</code> in the figure creation to have the title not be cropped when resizing.</p>
<p>A similar question about positioning the legend below the axes has been asked in <a href="https://stackoverflow.com/questions/55206575/how-to-get-constant-distance-between-legend-and-axes-even-when-the-figure-is-res">How to get constant distance between legend and axes even when the figure is resized?</a></p>
