<p>You didn't actually register your logger.  You need to instantiate your logger and then register it as a handler, something like:</p>
<pre><code>robot = WeReBot(...)  # make my handler
logger = logging.getLogger()  # get a reference to the root logger
logger.addHandler(robot)  # register my handler
</code></pre>
