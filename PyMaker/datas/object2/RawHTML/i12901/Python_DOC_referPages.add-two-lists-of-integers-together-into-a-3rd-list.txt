<pre><code>from operator import add
map(add, list1, list2)
</code></pre>
<p>Another would be:</p>
<pre><code>[x + y for x, y in zip(list1, list2)]
</code></pre>
<p>And yet another one is </p>
<pre><code>[sum(x) for x in zip(list1, list2)]
</code></pre>
