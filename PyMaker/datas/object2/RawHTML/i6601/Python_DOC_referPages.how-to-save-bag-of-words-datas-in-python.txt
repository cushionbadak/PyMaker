<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Use <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/pickle.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer">pickle</a> for this</p>
<ol>
<li><p>Save BOW to pickle:</p>
<pre><code>import pickle

sift=cv2.xfeatures2d.SIFT_create()
descriptors_unclustered=[]
dictionarysize=800
BOW=cv2.BOWKmeansTrainer(dictionarysize)
for p in training-paths :
    kp,dsc=sift.detectAndCompute(image,None)
    BOW.add(dsc)

with open('bow_pickle.pickle', 'wb') as f:
pickle.dump(f)
</code></pre></li>
<li><p>Return the data from pickle:</p>
<pre><code>import pickle    
with open('bow_pickle.pickle', 'rb') as f:
    BOW = pickle.load(f)    
dictionary=BOW.cluster()
bowdiction=cv2.BOWImgDescriptorExtractor(sift,cv2.BFMatcher(cv2.NORM_L2))
bowdiction.setvocabulary(dictionary)
</code></pre></li>
</ol>
