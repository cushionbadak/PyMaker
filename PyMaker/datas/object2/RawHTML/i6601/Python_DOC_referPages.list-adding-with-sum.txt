<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Go like this:</p>
<pre><code>sum([int(i) for i in list1])
</code></pre>
<p>Where:</p>
<pre><code>[int(i) for i in list1]
</code></pre>
<p>Converts your list to <code>int</code>, and <code>sum()</code> adds up those integers.</p>
<p>Another approach is using <code>map()</code>:</p>
<pre><code>sum(map(int, list1))
</code></pre>
<p>This also converts list1 into <code>int</code> and sums up the contents.</p>
<p>Finally you can <code>reduce()</code> your list using the anonymous function <code>lambda</code> that adds up elements converted to <code>int</code>:</p>
<pre><code>reduce(lambda x, y: int(x) + int(y), list1)
</code></pre>
</div>
