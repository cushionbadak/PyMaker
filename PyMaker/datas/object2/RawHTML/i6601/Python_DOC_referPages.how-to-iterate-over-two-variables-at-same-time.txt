<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You can use <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/functions.html#zip" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>zip(*iterables)</code></a>:</p>
<pre><code>for startDate, endDate in zip(startDates, endDates):
    print startDate
    print endDate
</code></pre>
<p>Output:</p>
<pre><code>2011-01-01
2013-01-01
2012-01-01
2014-01-01
</code></pre>
