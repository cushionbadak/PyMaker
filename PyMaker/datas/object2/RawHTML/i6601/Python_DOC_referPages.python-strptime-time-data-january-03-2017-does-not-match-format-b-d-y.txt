<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Take a closer look at the <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/datetime.html#strftime-and-strptime-behavior" rel="nofollow noreferrer">docs</a> (emphasis mine):</p>
<blockquote>
<p>%b - Month as locale’s <strong>abbreviated</strong> name. (Jan, Feb, …, Dec)</p>
<p>%B - Month as locale’s <strong>full</strong> name. (January, February, …, December)</p>
</blockquote>
<p>You want <code>'%B %d %Y'</code>, not <code>'%b %d %Y'</code>.</p>
</div>
