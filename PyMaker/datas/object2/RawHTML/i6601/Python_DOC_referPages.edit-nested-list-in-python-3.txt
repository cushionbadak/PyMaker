<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>But what type of data do you expect? By defining</p>
<pre><code>x = item1, item2
</code></pre>
<p>You get a tuple. You can convert list to tuple by</p>
<pre><code>servers = tuple(servers)
</code></pre>
<p>That gives you:</p>
<pre><code> (["10.10.10.10" , "20.20.20.20"] ,["30.30.30.30"] , ["40.40.40.40", "50.50.50.50"] , ["60.60.60.60"],["70.70.70.70"])
</code></pre>
<p>Or you can flatten you list:</p>
<pre><code>servers = [el for item in servers for el in item]
</code></pre>
<p>But then you will get:</p>
<pre><code>["10.10.10.10" , "20.20.20.20" ,"30.30.30.30" , "40.40.40.40", "50.50.50.50" , "60.60.60.60","70.70.70.70"]
