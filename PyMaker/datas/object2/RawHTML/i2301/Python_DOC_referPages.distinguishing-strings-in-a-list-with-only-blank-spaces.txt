<p>You need Python's <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/re.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer">re</a> for exact matches of spaces with regex.</p>
<pre><code>import re
with open('FilePath') as file:
contents = file.readlines()
regex = re.compile(r"^\s{8}&lt;\/Item&gt;")
#for 4 spaces you can replace 8 with 4


for index, item in enumerate(contents):
    if (bool(regex.search(item))):
        print (item)
        print('&lt;/Item&gt; with 8 spaces')
</code></pre>
<p>Output :</p>
<pre><code>        &lt;/Item&gt;

&lt;/Item&gt; with 8 spaces
</code></pre>
