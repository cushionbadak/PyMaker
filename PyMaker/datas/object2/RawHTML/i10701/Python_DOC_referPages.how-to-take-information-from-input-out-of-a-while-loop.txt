<p>using generators:</p>
<pre><code>names = [input("What is the name of your dog") for i in range(3)]
print(names)
</code></pre>
<p>using list:</p>
<pre><code>names = []

s = 5
while s &gt;= 3:
    name = input("What is the name of your dog")
    names.append(name)
    s = s - 1
</code></pre>
<p>using yield:</p>
<pre><code>def names():
    s = 5
    while s &gt;= 3:
        yield input("What is the name of your dog")
        s = s - 1

for name in names():
    print(name)

# or

print(list(names()))
</code></pre>
