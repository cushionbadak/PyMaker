<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>If you haven't assigned anything to <code>x</code> or <code>y</code> they don't exist and <code>Coordinates(x,y)</code> will produce a NameError.</p>
<p>You defined the <code>x</code> and <code>y</code> parameters with default values, try:</p>
<pre><code>p0 = Coordinates()
</code></pre>
<p>or</p>
<pre><code>x = 3
y = 2
p1 = Coordinates(x,y)
</code></pre>
<hr/>
<p>Even though you gave <code>x</code> and <code>y</code> default values <strong>in</strong> the class, they are not in the same scope as your <code>p0 = Coordinates(x,y)</code> statement.</p>
<p><a href="https://docs.python.org/3/reference/executionmodel.html#structure-of-a-program" rel="nofollow noreferrer">4.1 Structure of a program; 4.2 Naming and Binding.</a></p>
