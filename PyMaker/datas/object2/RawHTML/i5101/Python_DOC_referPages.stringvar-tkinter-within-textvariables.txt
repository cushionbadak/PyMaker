<p>You seem to understand why it outputs what it does, so I will not address that.
To print each variable individually, you can do:</p>
<pre><code>for var in v1list:
  print(var.get())
</code></pre>
<p>To print them all in 1 list, you can do:</p>
<pre><code>print([var.get() for var in v1list])
</code></pre>
<p>This uses a <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/tutorial/datastructures.html#list-comprehensions" rel="nofollow noreferrer">list comprehension</a>.</p>
