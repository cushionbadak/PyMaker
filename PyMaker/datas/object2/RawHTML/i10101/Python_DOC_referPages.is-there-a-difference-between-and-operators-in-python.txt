<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>The <a href="https://docs.python.org/2.7/reference/lexical_analysis.html#operators" rel="noreferrer">python documentation</a> says that they are equivalent. </p>
<blockquote>
<p>The comparison operators <code>&lt;&gt;</code> and <code>!=</code> are alternate spellings of the same operator. <code>!=</code> is the preferred spelling; <code>&lt;&gt;</code> is obsolescent.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>The <code>&lt;&gt;</code> operator has been <em>removed</em> from Python 3.</p>
