<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>In my application, I have to store timestamp of current time and same time in future after 1 year.</p>
<p>To get current time, I'm using</p>
<pre><code>import time
ct = int(time.time())
</code></pre>
<p><strong>How to time at present in future after 1 year?</strong></p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You should probably use the <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/datetime.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>datetime</code></a> module.</p>
<pre><code>from datetime import datetime, timedelta

now = datetime.utcnow()
future = now + timedelta(days=365)

now_timestamp = int(now.timestamp())
future_timestamp = int(future.timestamp())
</code></pre>
<p>If you have to consider leap years, I would recommend using the <a href="https://pendulum.eustace.io/" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>Pendulum</code></a> library.</p>
</div>
