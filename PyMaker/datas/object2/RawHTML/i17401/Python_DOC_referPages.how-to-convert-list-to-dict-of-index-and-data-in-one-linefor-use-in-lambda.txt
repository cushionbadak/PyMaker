<p>I have a list</p>
<pre><code>a = ['a','b','c','d']
</code></pre>
<p>I want to convert to following format</p>
<pre><code>{0:'a', 1:'b', 2:'c', 3:'d' }
</code></pre>
<p>Can somebody tell me how to do this ? </p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<pre><code>dict(enumerate(a))
{0: 'a', 1: 'b', 2: 'c', 3: 'd'}
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Use <a href="http://docs.python.org/3/library/functions.html#func-dict"><code>dict</code></a> and <a href="http://docs.python.org/3/library/functions.html#enumerate"><code>enumerate</code></a>:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; a = ['a','b','c','d']
&gt;&gt;&gt; dict(enumerate(a))
{0: 'a', 1: 'b', 2: 'c', 3: 'd'}
&gt;&gt;&gt;
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Learning about <code>dict</code> comprehension can be useful too:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt;a = ['a','b','c','d']
&gt;&gt;&gt;{k:v for k,v in enumerate(a)}
{0: 'a', 1: 'b', 2: 'c', 3: 'd'}
</code></pre>
</div>
