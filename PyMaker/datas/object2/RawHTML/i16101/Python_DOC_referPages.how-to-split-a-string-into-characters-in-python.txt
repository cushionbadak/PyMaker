<p><code>list('Hello world!')</code> gives what you want, not <code>['Hello', 'world!']</code>.</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; print(list('Hello world!'))
['H', 'e', 'l', 'l', 'o', ' ', 'w', 'o', 'r', 'l', 'd', '!']
</code></pre>
<p>I think you confused the output of <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/stdtypes.html#str.split"><code>str.split</code></a>:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; print('Hello world!'.split())
['Hello', 'world!']
</code></pre>
