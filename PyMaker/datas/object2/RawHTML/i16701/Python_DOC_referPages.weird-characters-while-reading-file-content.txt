<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Specifying <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/codecs.html#standard-encodings" rel="noreferrer">encoding</a> will solve your problem.</p>
<pre><code>for line in open(textfile, 'r', encoding='utf-8-sig'):
    print(line)
</code></pre>
<p><a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/codecs.html#module-encodings.utf_8_sig" rel="noreferrer"><code>utf_8_sig</code></a>: UTF-8 codec with BOM signature</p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You must set the encoding of your file while reading it, using UTF-8.</p>
<p>Add a third parameter to your code, setting its enconding. From:</p>
<pre><code>for line in open(textfile, 'r'):
    print(line)
</code></pre>
<p>to:</p>
<pre><code>for line in open(textfile, 'r', encoding='utf-8-sig'):
    print (line)
</code></pre>
