<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You're looking for:</p>
<pre><code>print([''.join(tup) for tup in permutations(original)])
</code></pre>
<p><code>permutations</code> returns an iterator (not a list) of tuples containing all the permutations of <code>original</code> (e.g. <code>('t', 'e', 'n'), ('t', 'n', 'e'), ...</code>). So you need to iterate over the iterator, and join each tuple in to a string.</p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>DSM (in the comments) is right that you want <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/itertools.html#itertools.permutations" rel="nofollow"><code>permutations</code></a> rather than <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/itertools.html#itertools.combinations" rel="nofollow"><code>combinations</code></a>.</p>
<p>As to your reported problem - like everything else in the <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/itertools.html" rel="nofollow"><code>itertools</code></a> module, <code>permutations</code> and <code>combinations</code> return <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/tutorial/classes.html#iterators" rel="nofollow">iterators</a>. So, you can either iterate over the result:</p>
<pre><code>for permuted in permutations(original):
    print(permuted)
</code></pre>
<p>... or convert it to a list:</p>
<pre><code>print(list(permutations(original)))
</code></pre>
</div>
