<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You misspelled <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/reference/datamodel.html#object.__init__" rel="nofollow"><code>__init__</code></a>, it has <strong>two</strong> underscores at the start and end (for a total of 4 underscores). Your <code>_init_</code> method is never called.</p>
