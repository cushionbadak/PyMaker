<pre><code>data=[['2', '33494.45', '91.415838', '0'], ['2', '33994.3', '101.1738', '0'], ['2','34494.15', '107.04941', '0'], ['2', '34994', '107.67817', '0'], ['2', '35493.85', '107.26302', '0'], ['2', '35500', '107.21114', '0'], ['2', '35993.7', '103.04895', '0']]

self.do_something(data[i])
</code></pre>

<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You can just do this:</p>
<pre><code>for item in data:
    self.do_something(item)
</code></pre>
<p>Take a look at the <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/tutorial/controlflow.html#for-statements" rel="nofollow noreferrer">documentation</a>.</p>
