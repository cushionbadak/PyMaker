<span class="comment-copy">Make sure your col has values in it. <code>if len(col) &gt; 0: print(col[1])</code></span>
<span class="comment-copy">(not so) wild guess: you have an empty line at the end of the file? when you do <code>i-split(';')</code> on it, since there is no <code>;</code>, you don't get a list with at least two elements, so <code>col[1]</code> fails.</span>
