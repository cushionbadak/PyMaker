<p>Is there is easy way to turn digits that represent the month and year into text?</p>
<p>So for example:</p>
<pre><code>1208
109
209
309
409
</code></pre>
<p>Would be:</p>
<pre><code>December 2008
January 2009
February 2009
March 2009
April 2009
</code></pre>
<p>The only way I can think of doing it currently is via a series of if/else statements.</p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Sure, the idea is to:</p>
<ul>
<li>convert the numbers to strings</li>
<li>use <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/datetime.html#datetime.datetime.strptime"><code>.strptime()</code></a> to load strings into datetime objects</li>
<li>use <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/datetime.html#datetime.datetime.strftime"><code>.strftime()</code></a> to dump datetimes back to strings in a desired format (<code>%B %Y</code> in your case)</li>
</ul>
<p>Demo:</p>
<pre><code>In [1]: l = [1208, 109, 209, 309, 409]

In [2]: [datetime.strptime(str(item), "%m%y").strftime("%B %Y") for item in l]
Out[2]: ['December 2008', 'January 2009', 'February 2009', 'March 2009', 'April 2009']
</code></pre>
