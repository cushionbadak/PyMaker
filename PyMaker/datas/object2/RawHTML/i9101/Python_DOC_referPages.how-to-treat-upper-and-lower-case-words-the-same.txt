<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>To compare two words ignoring case, simply convert them both to, e.g., lower case: <code>word1.lower() == word2.lower()</code>.</p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<blockquote>
<p>to treat the lower case and upper case words the same</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Use <code>str.lower()</code> function:</p>
<pre><code>for i, word in enumerate(words):
    if keyword.lower() == word.lower():
        print(i+1)
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You can use <a href="https://docs.python.org/2/library/stdtypes.html#str.upper" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>str.upper()</code></a> or <a href="https://docs.python.org/2/library/stdtypes.html#str.lower" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>str.lower()</code></a> to turn a string into either all uppercase or all lowercase respectively.</p>
</div>
