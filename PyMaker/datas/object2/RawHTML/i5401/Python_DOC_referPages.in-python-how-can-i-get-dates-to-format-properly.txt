<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>The correct format is <code>%m/%d/%Y %H:%M</code> (note, no <code>$</code>, the <code>Y</code> is capitalised to indicate 4 digit year format).</p>
<pre><code>In [4]: datetime.strptime('11/06/2017 17:59',"%m/%d/%Y %H:%M")
Out[4]: datetime.datetime(2017, 11, 6, 17, 59)
</code></pre>
<p>For more information on various legal datetime formats, visit the <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/datetime.html#strftime-and-strptime-behavior" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>datetime</code> documentation.</a></p>
<hr/>
<p>For your second bit, use <code>dt.date</code> and <code>dt.time</code> to grab individual components.</p>
<pre><code>In [5]: d.date()
Out[5]: datetime.date(2017, 11, 6)

In [6]: d.time()
Out[6]: datetime.time(17, 59)
</code></pre>
<p>You can call <code>str</code> on the result after, for a human readable output.</p>
