<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Use <code>str.replace()</code>:</p>
<pre><code>s = 'sanmayın.mp3'
s.replace('ı','i')
</code></pre>
<p><strong>Result</strong>:</p>
<pre><code>sanmayin.mp3
</code></pre>
<p>If you needed to replace characters within a list:</p>
<pre><code>l = ['sanmayın.mp3', 'ın', 'hı']
l = [i.replace('ı','i') for i in l] 
</code></pre>
<p><strong>Result</strong>:</p>
<pre><code>['sanmayin.mp3', 'in', 'hi']
</code></pre>
