<p>You want <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/itertools.html#itertools.combinations_with_replacement" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>combinations_with_replacement()</code></a> instead:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; from itertools import combinations_with_replacement
&gt;&gt;&gt; list(combinations_with_replacement(x, 2))
[(1, 1), (1, 2), (1, 3), (1, 4), (1, 5), (2, 2), (2, 3), (2, 4), (2, 5), (3, 3), (3, 4), (3, 5), (4, 4), (4, 5), (5, 5)]
