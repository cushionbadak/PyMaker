<p>Change the display option for floats:</p>
<pre><code>import pandas as pd

pd.options.display.float_format = '{:.2f}'.format

print(df)
#                          min             max            mean
#marketCap(EUR) 61103310000.00 283742900000.00 127599300000.00
#price(EUR)            3680.00        16943.95         7538.25
#generatedCoins        1000.00         2437.50         1921.52
#paymentCount        238637.00       808680.00       401912.70
</code></pre>
<p>It seems like you also want to disply the first row in billions, so do:</p>
<pre><code>df.loc['marketCap(EUR)'] = df.loc['marketCap(EUR)']/10**9

#                     min       max      mean
#marketCap(EUR)     61.10    283.74    127.60
#price(EUR)       3680.00  16943.95   7538.25
#generatedCoins   1000.00   2437.50   1921.52
#paymentCount   238637.00 808680.00 401912.70
</code></pre>
<p>Or perhaps don't change the data and add <code>,</code> to separate every 3 orders of magintude:</p>
<pre><code>pd.options.display.float_format = '{:,.2f}'.format

#                             min                max               mean
#marketCap(EUR) 61,103,310,000.00 283,742,900,000.00 127,599,300,000.00
