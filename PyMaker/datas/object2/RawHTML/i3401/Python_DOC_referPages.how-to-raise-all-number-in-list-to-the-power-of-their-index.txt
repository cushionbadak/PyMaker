<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Use <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/functions.html#enumerate" rel="nofollow noreferrer">enumerate</a> in the <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/tutorial/datastructures.html#list-comprehensions" rel="nofollow noreferrer">list comprehension</a> like:</p>
<h3>Code:</h3>
<pre><code>[x ** i for i, x in enumerate(data)]
</code></pre>
<h3>Test Code:</h3>
<pre><code>data = [2, 2, 2, 2, 2, 2]

print([x ** i for i, x in enumerate(data)])
</code></pre>
<h3>Results:</h3>
<pre><code>[1, 2, 4, 8, 16, 32]
</code></pre>
