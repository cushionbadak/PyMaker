<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>everyone, I have a data frame such as : </p>
<pre><code>groups  name
1   A
1   B
1   C
1   D
2   E
3   F
3   G
4   H
5   I
</code></pre>
<p>and from that I would like to only keep in the data frame the values that are alone in a group:</p>
<pre><code>groups  name
2   E
4   H
5   I
</code></pre>
<p>E,H and I are alone in their respective groups. </p>
<p>I tried: </p>
<pre><code>df[df.groupby(['groups']).count() == 1 ]
</code></pre>
<p>But it does not seem to be the solution. </p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Use <a href="http://pandas.pydata.org/pandas-docs/stable/reference/api/pandas.core.groupby.GroupBy.transform.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>GroupBy.transform</code></a> for Series with same size like original <code>DataFrame</code>:</p>
<pre><code>df[df.groupby(['groups'])['name'].transform('size') == 1 ]
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Use <code>duplicated</code>:</p>
<pre><code>df[~df.groups.duplicated(keep=False)]

   groups name
4       2    E
7       4    H
8       5    I
</code></pre>
<p>Or, <code>drop_duplicates</code>.</p>
<pre><code>df.drop_duplicates('groups', keep=False)

   groups name
4       2    E
7       4    H
8       5    I
</code></pre>
</div>
