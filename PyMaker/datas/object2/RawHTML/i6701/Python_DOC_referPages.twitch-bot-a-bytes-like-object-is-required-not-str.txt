<p>A string is an abstraction, representing a sequence of unicode codepoints.
To turn a string into a sequence of bytes, you need to encode your string, aka decide on how you want to represent your text on the wire. For Twitch, use UTF-8:</p>
<pre><code>full_msg = "PRIVMSG #{} :{}".format(cfg.CHAN, msg)
msg_encoded = full_msg.encode("utf-8")
sock.send(msg_encoded)
