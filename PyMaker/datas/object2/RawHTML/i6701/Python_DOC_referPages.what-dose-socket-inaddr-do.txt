<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Those constants refer to <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IP_address" rel="nofollow noreferrer">IP addresses</a>. Refer to your system Unix documentation for what they mean, as indicated in the <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/socket.html#socket.SOMAXCONN" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Python documentation</a>. I don't think <code>socket.INADDR_LOOPBAD</code> or <code>socket.INADDR_RESERVED</code> are real constants.</p>
<p>A few common constants are:</p>
<ul>
<li><code>INADDR_LOOPBACK</code> (127.0.0.1) - your local host, via a special network device known as the loopback device</li>
<li><code>INADDR_ANY</code> (0.0.0.0) - any address used for binding (accept connections to any IP on the machine)</li>
</ul>
<p>The <a href="http://man7.org/linux/man-pages/man7/ip.7.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer">IP manual</a> has more useful information on these constants.</p>
