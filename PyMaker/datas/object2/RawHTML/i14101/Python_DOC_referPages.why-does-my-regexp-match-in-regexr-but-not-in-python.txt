<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Because you should replace <code>match</code> with <code>search</code>. </p>
<p><code>match</code> tries to match the string from the beginning, and <code>\"O1533_FOO\" INTEGER NOT NULL ,</code> doesn't begin with something that matches <code>[A-Z]</code>, hence it fails.</p>
<p>See <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/re.html#search-vs-match"><code>search()</code> vs. <code>match()</code></a>:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>Python offers two different primitive operations based on regular expressions: <code>re.match()</code> checks for a <strong>match only at the beginning of the string</strong>, while <code>re.search()</code> <strong>checks for a match anywhere in the string</strong> (this is what Perl does by default).</p>
</blockquote>
</div>
<span class="comment-copy">Because <code>re.match</code> match the string form beginning . you need <code>re.search</code>.</span>
