y2 = np.linspace(10,1)

c = ["b","g"]

ax2.plot(y2, c[1])

for tl in ax.yaxis.get_ticklines():
    tl.set_color(c[0])

for ts in ax.yaxis.get_ticklabels():
    ts.set_color(c[0])

<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>I guess, there are twice as many <code>ax.yaxis.get_ticklines()</code> than there are <code>ax.yaxis.get_ticklabels()</code>, so zip just stops before painting them all, while individual loops are fine.</p>
<p>This behaviour of <code>zip</code> is explained in the <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/functions.html#zip" rel="nofollow">Python documentation</a>.</p>
