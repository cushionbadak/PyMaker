<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You wanted this instead:</p>
<pre><code>datetime.datetime.strptime(userBirthday, "%d/%m/%Y")
</code></pre>
<p>Where m stands for "month".</p>
<p>Also, checkout <code>dateutil.parser.parse</code>, which can recognise a wide variety of formats.</p>
