<p>Right, that's the default type of the result of the <code>.fetchall()</code> - these are actually not tuples but <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/sqlite3.html#sqlite3.Row" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>Row</code> objects</a> which mimic the tuples. You could <em>unpack them in the loop</em>:</p>
<pre><code>for url, in mylist_of_url:
    mylist_of_url_splitted.append(url.split('/'))
</code></pre>
