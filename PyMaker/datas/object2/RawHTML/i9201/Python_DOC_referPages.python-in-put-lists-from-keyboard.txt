<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You initialized list1 to an empty list</p>
<pre><code>list1 = []
</code></pre>
<p>In order to add a new item to the end of the list use append()</p>
<pre><code>for i in range(0,6):
   list1.append(input("enter a integer of list1:"))
</code></pre>
<p>In your example</p>
<pre><code>for i in range(0,6):
   list1[i] = input("enter a integer of list1:")
</code></pre>
<p>You were getting an IndexError because you were trying to access an index in list1 that doesnt exist, since the length of an empty list is zero.</p>
<p>(FYI this answer is specific to your error, not the rest of your code)</p>
<p>Docs:<a href="https://docs.python.org/3/tutorial/datastructures.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer">https://docs.python.org/3/tutorial/datastructures.html</a></p>
