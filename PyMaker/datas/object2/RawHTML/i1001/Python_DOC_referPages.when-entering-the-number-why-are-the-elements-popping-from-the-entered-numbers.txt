<p>The <code>list.pop(i)</code> removes i-th element of the list by it's index. If you need to remove element by value, use <code>list.remove(x)</code> method instead.</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; a = [10, 11, 12, 12]
&gt;&gt;&gt; a.pop(1)
11
&gt;&gt;&gt; a
[10, 12, 12]
&gt;&gt;&gt; a.remove(12)
&gt;&gt;&gt; a
[10, 12]
</code></pre>
