<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p><code>parse_graph6</code> is renamed to <code>from_graph6_bytes</code> as seen in this <a href="https://github.com/networkx/networkx/commit/5c22e401bdf5fdff19147bd1328a934f8d3205b5?diff=unified#diff-702b35c7ef6d54af79be44bd6f64b9e7R74" rel="nofollow noreferrer">diff</a>.</p>
<p>Going forward in versions of networkx greater than <strong>2.0</strong>, <code>from_graph6_bytes</code> is used.</p>
