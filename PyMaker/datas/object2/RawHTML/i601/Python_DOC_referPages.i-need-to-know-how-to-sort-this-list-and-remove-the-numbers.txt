<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You can use the <code>filter</code> function with <code>str.isalpha</code> as the test condition on the characters in the strings in <code>x</code>:</p>
<pre><code>sorted((''.join(filter(str.isalpha, s)) for s in x))
</code></pre>
<p>This returns:</p>
<pre><code>['barcelona', 'bogota', 'chennai', 'hyderabad', 'istanbul']
</code></pre>
