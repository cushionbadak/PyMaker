<pre><code>[[1,2,3],[4,5,6],[7,8,9]]
</code></pre>
<p>would turn into:</p>
<pre><code>[[1,4,7],[2,5,8],[3,6,9]]
</code></pre>
<p>I know my list has a fixed 4rows*4columns. After I switch, I will have a function that manipulates it then switches is back.</p>

<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Use the common <code>zip</code> idiom:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; zip(*[[1,2,3],[4,5,6],[7,8,9]])
[(1, 4, 7), (2, 5, 8), (3, 6, 9)]
</code></pre>
