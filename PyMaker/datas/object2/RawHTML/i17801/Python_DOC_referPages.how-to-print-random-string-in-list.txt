<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Use <a href="http://docs.python.org/3/library/random.html#random.shuffle" rel="nofollow"><code>random.shuffle()</code></a> to shuffle your list in place. For example</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; a = ['hello','chicken','world']
&gt;&gt;&gt; import random
&gt;&gt;&gt; a
['hello', 'chicken', 'world']
&gt;&gt;&gt; random.shuffle(a)
&gt;&gt;&gt; a
['chicken', 'world', 'hello']
&gt;&gt;&gt; random.shuffle(a)
&gt;&gt;&gt; a
['hello', 'world', 'chicken']
</code></pre>
