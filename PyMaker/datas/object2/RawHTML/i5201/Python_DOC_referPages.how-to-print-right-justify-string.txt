<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You should be using <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/stdtypes.html#str.rjust" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>str.rjust</code></a> to right justify your string. For example</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; '*'.rjust(5)
'    *'
</code></pre>
<p>Using this, you may create your function as:</p>
<pre><code>def print_tree(height):
    half_height = int((height+1)/2)
    for i in range(1, height+1):
        if i &lt;= half_height:
            print(('*'*i).rjust(half_height))
        else:
            print(('*'*(height-i+1)).rjust(half_height))
</code></pre>
<p>Sample Run:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; print_tree(5)
  *
 **
***
 **
  *
&gt;&gt;&gt; print_tree(4)
 *
**
**
 *
&gt;&gt;&gt; print_tree(3)
 *
**
 *
</code></pre>
