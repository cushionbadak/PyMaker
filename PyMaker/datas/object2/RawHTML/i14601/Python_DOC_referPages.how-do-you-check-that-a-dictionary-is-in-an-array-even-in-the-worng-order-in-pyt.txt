<p>Lets say you have :</p>
<pre><code> winning_numbers = [{345}, {238}, {321} ]

 players_numbers = {435}
</code></pre>
<p>I want to return True that players_numbers is in the the array even if its not in the right order. How can I tackle doing this?</p>

<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You should make your winning numbers into a <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/tutorial/datastructures.html#sets" rel="nofollow">set</a></p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; winning_numbers = [ {3, 4, 5}, {2, 3, 8}, {3, 2, 1} ]
&gt;&gt;&gt; players_numbers = {4, 3, 5}
&gt;&gt;&gt; players_numbers in winning_numbers
True
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p><em>sets</em> are of the form</p>
<pre><code>{value1... valueX}
</code></pre>
<p>a <em>dictionary</em> is actually</p>
<pre><code>{key1:value1... keyX:valueX}
</code></pre>
<p>As for your question, you could simply use the <strong>in</strong> keyword:</p>
<pre><code>if players_numbers in winning_numbers:
    print "you have a winner!"
</code></pre>
</div>
