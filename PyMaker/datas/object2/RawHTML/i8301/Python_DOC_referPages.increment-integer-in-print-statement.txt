<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Clearly you are really wanting to use <code>enumerate</code> to solve your problem.  But to answer the specific question of "how can I increment <code>i</code> within the <code>print</code> statement" ... then you can do the following <strong>very ugly thing</strong> (its not strictly <em>within</em>):</p>
<pre><code>i=0
for item in splitDict(Team, 3):
    i += print("{1} #{0}".format(i + 1, item)) or 1
</code></pre>
<p>But you shouldn't.  Use <code>enumerate</code>:</p>
<pre><code>for i, item in enumerate(splitDict(Team, 3)):
    print("{1} #{0}".format(i, item))
</code></pre>
