<p>Straight from your own link.</p>
<p>(<a href="https://docs.python.org/3/distutils/setupscript.html#listing-whole-packages" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Relevant section</a>)</p>
<blockquote>
<p>The <code>packages</code> option tells the Distutils to process (build, distribute, install, etc.) all pure Python modules found in each package mentioned in the <code>packages</code> list.</p>
<p>...</p>
<p>Thus, when you say <code>packages = ['foo']</code> in your setup script, you are promising that the Distutils will find a file <code>foo/__init__.py</code></p>
