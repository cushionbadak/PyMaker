<p>What is the value 0 in front of [].</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt;somelist = list('SPAM')

&gt;&gt;&gt;'first = {0[0]}, third = {0[2]}' .format(somelist)

'first=S, third=A'
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p><code>0</code> represents the first argument of the <code>format</code> function, which is <code>somelist</code>.</p>
<p>This means that this <code>0</code> will be replaced by <code>somelist</code> and then its first (<code>[0]</code>) and third (<code>[2]</code>) elements are used in the string.</p>
<p>Therefore <code>0</code> is <code>['S', 'P', 'A', 'M']</code>, <code>0[0]</code> is <code>'S'</code> and <code>0[2]</code> is <code>'A'</code>.</p>
