<p>Although you generally just see it being used with the stop parameter alone, the <code>range()</code> function actually has start, stop, and step parameters. e.g.</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; for i in range(6, 20, 3): print(i)
6
9
12
15
18
</code></pre>
<p>So just gather the starting value, and calculate the stop parameter from the start value and the number of multiples desired, and fix the step parameter at three.</p>
