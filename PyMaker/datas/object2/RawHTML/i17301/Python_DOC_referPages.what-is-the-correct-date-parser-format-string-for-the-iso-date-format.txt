<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>From the <a href="http://docs.python.org/2/library/datetime.html#strftime-and-strptime-behavior" rel="nofollow">docs</a> (emphasis mine):</p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>%y</strong> Year <strong>without century</strong> as a zero-padded decimal number.   00, 01, ..., 99<br/>
<strong>%Y</strong>  Year <strong>with century</strong> as a decimal number.  1970, 1988, 2001, 2013   </p>
</blockquote>
<p>Therefore <code>%y-%m-%d</code> is expecting <code>14-01-03</code> while you have <code>2014-01-03</code> which requires <code>%Y-%m-%d</code>.</p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Difference between upper and lower case:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; datetime.datetime.strptime('2014-01-03', '%Y-%m-%d').date()
datetime.date(2014, 1, 3)
&gt;&gt;&gt; datetime.datetime.strptime('14-01-03', '%y-%m-%d').date()
datetime.date(2014, 1, 3)
</code></pre>
<p>Documented in <a href="http://docs.python.org/3/library/datetime.html#strftime-strptime-behavior" rel="nofollow">strptime and strftime behavior</a>.</p>
</div>
