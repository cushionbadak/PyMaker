<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p><code>namedtuple</code> objects don't accept a list of <code>(attribute, type)</code> tuples, they just expect an iterable of attributes (or a single string):</p>
<pre><code>namedtuple('dash_data', ('posting', 'folder'))
</code></pre>
<p>Or:</p>
<pre><code>namedtuple('dash_data', 'posting folder')
</code></pre>
<p>Refer to <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/collections.html#collections.namedtuple" rel="nofollow noreferrer">the documentation</a> for how to use <code>namedtuple</code>.</p>
</div>
