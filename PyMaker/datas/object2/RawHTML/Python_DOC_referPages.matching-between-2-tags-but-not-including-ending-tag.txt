<p>Use <a href="https://docs.python.org/2/library/re.html#re.S" rel="nofollow"><code>re.S</code></a> or <a href="https://docs.python.org/2/library/re.html#re.DOTALL" rel="nofollow"><code>re.DOTALL</code></a> flags. Or prepend the regular expression with <code>(?s)</code> to make <code>.</code> matches all character (including newline).</p>
<p>Without the flags, <code>.</code> does not match newline.</p>
<pre><code>(?s)(?&lt;=Test)(.*?)(?=&lt;/td&gt;)
</code></pre>
<hr/>
<p>Example:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; s = '''&lt;td scope="row" align="left"&gt;
...       My Class: TEST DATA&lt;br&gt;
...       Test Section: &lt;br&gt;
...       MY SECTION&lt;br&gt;
...       MY SECTION 2&lt;br&gt;
...     &lt;/td&gt;'''
&gt;&gt;&gt;
&gt;&gt;&gt; import re
&gt;&gt;&gt; re.findall('(?&lt;=Test)(.*?)(?=&lt;/td&gt;)', s)  # without flags
[]
&gt;&gt;&gt; re.findall('(?&lt;=Test)(.*?)(?=&lt;/td&gt;)', s, flags=re.S)
[' Section: &lt;br&gt;\n      MY SECTION&lt;br&gt;\n      MY SECTION 2&lt;br&gt;\n    ']
&gt;&gt;&gt; re.findall('(?s)(?&lt;=Test)(.*?)(?=&lt;/td&gt;)', s)
[' Section: &lt;br&gt;\n      MY SECTION&lt;br&gt;\n      MY SECTION 2&lt;br&gt;\n    ']
</code></pre>

<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Get the matched group from index 1</p>
<pre><code>Test Section:([\S\s]*)&lt;/td&gt;
</code></pre>
<p><a href="http://regex101.com/r/zZ5xQ0/2" rel="nofollow">Live demo</a></p>
<p><strong>Note:</strong> change the last part as per your need.</p>
<p>sample code:</p>
<pre><code>import re
p = re.compile(ur'Test Section:([\S\s]*)&lt;/td&gt;', re.MULTILINE)
test_str = u"..."

re.findall(p, test_str)
</code></pre>
<p>Pattern Explanation:</p>
<pre><code>  Test Section:            'Test Section:'
  (                        group and capture to \1:
    [\S\s]*                  any character of: non-whitespace (all
                             but \n, \r, \t, \f, and " "), whitespace
                             (\n, \r, \t, \f, and " ") (0 or more
                             times (matching the most amount
                             possible))
  )                        end of \1
  &lt;/td&gt;                    '&lt;/td&gt;'
</code></pre>
