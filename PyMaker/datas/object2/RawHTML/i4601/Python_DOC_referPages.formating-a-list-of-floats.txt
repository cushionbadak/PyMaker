<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You are missing a <code>%</code> in your original code, which is why it doesnt work. Use <code>float("%.02f" % x)</code> instead of <code>float(".02f" % x)</code>:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; e =[0.2941, 0.1176, 0.0588, 0.2352, 0.1176, 0.1764]
&gt;&gt;&gt; print([float("%.02f" % x)  for x in e])
#                 ^the % is missing
[0.29, 0.12, 0.06, 0.24, 0.12, 0.18]
</code></pre>
<p>Alternatively, use <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/functions.html#round" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>round</code></a>:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; print([ round(x,2)  for x in e])
[0.29, 0.12, 0.06, 0.24, 0.12, 0.18]
</code></pre>

<p>you should use round(x,2) for rounding to desired level  </p>
<pre><code>e =[0.2941, 0.1176, 0.0588, 0.2352, 0.1176, 0.1764]
print([float("{0}".format(round(x,2)))  for x in e])
</code></pre>
