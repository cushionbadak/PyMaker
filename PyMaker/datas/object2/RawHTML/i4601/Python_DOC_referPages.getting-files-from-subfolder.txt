<p>Two parts to this:</p>
<p>1) You're going the wrong way. <code>../images/</code> goes up one directory. You just want <code>images/</code>. For absolute referencing you want <code>/face-recognition/images</code></p>
<p>2) <code>glob</code> is your friend here <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/glob.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer">https://docs.python.org/3/library/glob.html</a> </p>
<pre><code>import glob 
file_list = glob.glob('/face-recognition/images/*.png')
</code></pre>
<p>or whatever extension you need.</p>
