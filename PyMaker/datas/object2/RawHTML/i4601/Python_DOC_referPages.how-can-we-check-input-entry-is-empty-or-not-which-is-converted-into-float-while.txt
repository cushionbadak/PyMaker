<p>i'd go with a <code>try/except</code> clause:</p>
<pre><code>while(True):
    try:
        assign1=float(input("Please enter the marks: "))
    except:
        continue
    if(assign1&gt;100 or assign1&lt;0):
        continue
    else:
        marks=(assign1*20)/100
        break
</code></pre>
<p>In this way, anything that raise an error when converted to float (including '', or <em>Enter without giving any input</em>) will result in he repetition of the question.</p>
