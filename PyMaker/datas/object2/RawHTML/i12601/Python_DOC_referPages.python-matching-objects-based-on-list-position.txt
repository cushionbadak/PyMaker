<p>Use <code>enumerate()</code>:</p>
<pre><code>def iterationTest():
    for item in l:
        for idx, lis in enumerate(item):
            if idx in d['Client1']['file1DataPositions']:
                print('Match Found')

iterationTest()
</code></pre>
<p>This will iterate over each <code>lis</code> in <code>item</code>, and at each stage in the loop it will provide access to both <code>lis</code> and its index, <code>idx</code> (which you use for comparison).</p>
