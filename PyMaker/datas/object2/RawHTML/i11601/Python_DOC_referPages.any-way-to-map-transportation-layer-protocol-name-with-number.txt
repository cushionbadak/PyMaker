<p>You may be looking for <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/socket.html#socket.getprotobyname" rel="nofollow"><code>socket.getprotobyname</code></a>.</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; import socket
&gt;&gt;&gt; socket.getprotobyname('tcp')
6
&gt;&gt;&gt; socket.getprotobyname('udp')
17
&gt;&gt;&gt; socket.getprotobyname('icmp')
1
</code></pre>
