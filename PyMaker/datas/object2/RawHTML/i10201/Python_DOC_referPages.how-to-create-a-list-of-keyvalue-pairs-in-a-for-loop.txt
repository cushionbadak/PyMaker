<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>If you just want to make a dict from two lists, then use <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/functions.html#zip" rel="nofollow"><code>zip</code></a> function:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; keys, values = ['a', 'b'], [1, 2]
&gt;&gt;&gt; list(zip(keys, values))
[('a', 1), ('b', 2)]

&gt;&gt;&gt; dict(zip(keys, values))
{'a': 1, 'b': 2}

&gt;&gt;&gt; [{k: v} for k, v in zip(keys, values)]
[{'a': 1}, {'b': 2}]

&gt;&gt;&gt; ' '.join('{}:{}'.format(k, v) for k, v in zip(keys, values))
'a:1 b:2'

&gt;&gt;&gt; '[{}]'.format(', '.join("'{}': '{}'".format(k, v) for k, v in zip(keys, values)))
"['a': '1', 'b': '2']"
</code></pre>
