<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p><a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/functions.html#hex" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>hex</code></a> removes any leading zeros. If you want to maintain a length of 4 characters in the string, try doing <code>hex(SMI)[2:].zfill(4)</code> where it will ad leading zeros until it reaches the specified length.</p>
