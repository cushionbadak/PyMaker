<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You are looking for <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/itertools.html#itertools.product" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>itertools.product</code></a>:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; max, order = 3, 2
&gt;&gt;&gt; print(list(itertools.product(range(max + 1), repeat=order)))
[(0, 0), (0, 1), (0, 2), (0, 3),
 (1, 0), (1, 1), (1, 2), (1, 3),
 (2, 0), (2, 1), (2, 2), (2, 3),
 (3, 0), (3, 1), (3, 2), (3, 3)]
</code></pre>
