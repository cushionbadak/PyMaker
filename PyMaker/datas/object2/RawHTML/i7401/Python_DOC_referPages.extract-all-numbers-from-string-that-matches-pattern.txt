<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>It's not clear exactly what you want to consider a "match" or not, so I've made the assumption to consider any continuous series of digits with an underscore somewhere in the middle a match.</p>
<p>To find all matches in a string, you can use <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/re.html#re.findall" rel="nofollow noreferrer">re.findall</a>. Here's a demo:</p>
<pre><code>import re

s = "geod rfff eef 234_1538 ffgg df 134774  234_1645"

print(re.findall('\d+_\d+', s))
</code></pre>
<p><strong>Output</strong></p>
<pre><code>['234_1538', '234_1645']
</code></pre>
