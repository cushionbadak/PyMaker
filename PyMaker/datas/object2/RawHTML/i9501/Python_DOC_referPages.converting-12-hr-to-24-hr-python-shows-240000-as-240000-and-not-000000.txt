<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Use modulo arithmetic to divide by 24 and get the remainder. Change this:</p>
<pre><code>int(timestamp[:2]) + 12
</code></pre>
<p>to this:</p>
<pre><code>(int(timestamp[:2]) + 12) % 24
</code></pre>
<p>Demonstration:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; 12 + 12
24
&gt;&gt;&gt; (12 + 12) % 24
0
</code></pre>
