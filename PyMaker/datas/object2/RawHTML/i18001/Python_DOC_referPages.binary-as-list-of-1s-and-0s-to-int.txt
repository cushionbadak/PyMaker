<p>I would like to convert a list of integer 1s and 0s which represent a binary number to an int.</p>
<p>something along the lines of:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; [1,1,0,1].toint()
</code></pre>
<p>would give an output of <code>13</code></p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Strings are unnecessary here:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; l = [1,1,0,1]
&gt;&gt;&gt; 
&gt;&gt;&gt; sum(j&lt;&lt;i for i,j in enumerate(reversed(l)))
13
</code></pre>
