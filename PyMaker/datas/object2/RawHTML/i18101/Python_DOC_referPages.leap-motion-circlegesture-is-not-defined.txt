<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>I do not know the module, and without seeing your code this is just a guess, but from your question I infer that you use <a href="https://developer.leapmotion.com/documentation/Languages/Python/API/index.html" rel="nofollow">this Leap Motion API</a>, and did something like <code>import Leap</code> in your code.</p>
<p>This way, you do not yet have the <code>CircleGesture</code> class in your name space, but you have to access it <a href="http://docs.python.org/3/tutorial/modules.html" rel="nofollow">from within the <code>Leap</code> module</a>, just like you did with <code>Leap.Gesture.TYPE_CIRCLE</code>.</p>
<p>Try this: <code>circle = Leap.CircleGesture(gesture)</code></p>
<p>(as a side note: in Python, you do not have to terminate your lines with <code>;</code>)</p>
