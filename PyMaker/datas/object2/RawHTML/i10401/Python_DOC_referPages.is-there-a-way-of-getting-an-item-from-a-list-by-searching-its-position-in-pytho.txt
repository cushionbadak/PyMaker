<p>Input:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>print(words.index(2))</p>

<p>Output:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>I'm</p>
</blockquote>

<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You could just use the following syntax to access a certain element of your list:</p>
<pre><code>words[1]
</code></pre>
<p>Output:</p>
<pre><code>I'm
</code></pre>
<p>Note that the index starts with 0.</p>
