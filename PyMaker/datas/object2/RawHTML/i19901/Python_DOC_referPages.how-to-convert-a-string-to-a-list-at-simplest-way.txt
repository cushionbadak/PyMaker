<p>Simplest one I can think of:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt; list('ABCD')
['A', 'B', 'C', 'D']
</code></pre>

<li><a href="http://docs.python.org/2/library/functions.html#list" rel="nofollow">Python 2 Built-in Functions: list([<em>iterable</em>])</a></li>

<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Depending on your use case, you may not even have to do that - a string is already iterable:</p>
<pre><code>for c in "ABCD"
    print c
</code></pre>
<p>prints</p>
<pre><code>A
B
C
D
</code></pre>
