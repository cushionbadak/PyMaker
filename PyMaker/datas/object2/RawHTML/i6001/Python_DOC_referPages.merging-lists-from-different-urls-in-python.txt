<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Declare a list to hold all your numbers:</p>
<pre><code>data = []
</code></pre>
<p>Inside the loop, call <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/tutorial/datastructures.html#more-on-lists" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>list.extend</code></a>, to add every element of <code>events_per_hour</code> to <code>data</code> in one call:</p>
<pre><code>for dag in range(1, 32):
    ... 
    data.extend(events_per_hour)
</code></pre>
<p>At the end, <code>data</code> holds all your numbers as a single list.</p>

</code></pre>
