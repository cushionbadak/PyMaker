<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>I have saved a time as <code>presentTime=datetime.datetime.utcnow()</code></p>
<p>It 's output is 2014-08-18 21:11:35.537000. How can this be formatted to : August 18 2014 - 21:11:35 instead?</p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p><a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/datetime.html#datetime.datetime.utcnow" rel="noreferrer"><code>datetime.utcnow</code></a> returns a <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/datetime.html#datetime-objects" rel="noreferrer"><code>datetime</code></a> object. You can use its <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/datetime.html#datetime.datetime.strftime" rel="noreferrer"><code>strftime</code></a> method to convert it into a string of your desired format:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; datetime.datetime.utcnow().strftime('%B %d %Y - %H:%M:%S')
'August 20 2014 - 13:55:49'
</code></pre>
</div>
