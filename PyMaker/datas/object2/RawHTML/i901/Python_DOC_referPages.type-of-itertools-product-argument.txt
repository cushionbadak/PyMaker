<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Compare with <code>itertools.chain</code></p>
<pre><code>a = [1,2,3,4]
b = [5,6,7,8]
c = [a, b]

itertools.chain(a, b)             # 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
itertools.chain(c)                # [1, 2, 3, 4] [5, 6, 7, 8]

itertools.chain(*c)               # 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
# or chain specifically has a more-legible version of this
itertools.chain.from_iterable(c)  # 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
</code></pre>
<p>Note that <code>some_function(*[a, b, c])</code> is the same as <code>some_function(a, b, c)</code>. This is called argument unpacking.</p>
