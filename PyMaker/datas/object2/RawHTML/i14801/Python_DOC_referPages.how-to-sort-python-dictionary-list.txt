<p>Assuming I understood your question, these should do it:</p>
<pre><code>for person in sorted(cat1):
    print(person, max(cat1.get(person)))
</code></pre>
<p>result:</p>
<pre><code>ben 9
jeff 6
sam 9
</code></pre>
<p>then:</p>
<pre><code>for person in sorted(cat1, key=lambda x: max(cat1.get(x)), reverse=True):
    print(person, max(cat1.get(person)))
</code></pre>
<p>result:</p>
<pre><code>ben 9
sam 9
jeff 6
</code></pre>
<p>then:</p>
<pre><code>for person in sorted(cat1, key=lambda x: sum(cat1.get(x))/len(cat1.get(x)), reverse=True):
    print(person, sum(cat1.get(person))/len(cat1.get(person)))
</code></pre>
<p>result:</p>
<pre><code>ben 7.666666666666667
sam 6.666666666666667
jeff 4.0
</code></pre>
