<p>There are two things wrong.</p>
<p>for <code>'%m-%B-%Y-%H-%M'</code>, your date should be: <code>'12-October-2014-18-30'</code></p>
<p>In some you have <code>12th</code> where it should just be <code>12</code> and in all of them you have <code>18:30</code> but need it to be <code>18-30</code></p>
