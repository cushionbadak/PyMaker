<span class="comment-copy">What is in <code>Time</code> and how is it being used (or desired to be used)? The <code>datetime</code> constructor can not make a <code>datetime</code> from a format string, or any string for that matter. Take a look at <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/datetime.html#datetime.datetime.strptime" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>datetime.strptime()</code></a> to parse a time string into a <code>datetime.datetime</code> object.</span>
<span class="comment-copy">Also, your date string contains no timezone information, so converting to UTC makes little sense.</span>
