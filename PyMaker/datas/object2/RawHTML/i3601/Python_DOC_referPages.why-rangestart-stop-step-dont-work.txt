<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>I don't know where you got that message. <code>range</code> does not take keyword arguments, and the error is explicit about that:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; range(start=1, stop=3, step=2)
TypeError: range() does not take keyword arguments
</code></pre>
<p>Just use positional arguments:</p>
<pre><code>range(0, len(l), 10240)
</code></pre>
</div>
