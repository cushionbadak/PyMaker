<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>There is no direct way to get opening and closing parts of the tag in <code>BeautifulSoup</code>, but, at least, you can get the <a href="https://www.crummy.com/software/BeautifulSoup/bs4/doc/#name" rel="nofollow noreferrer">name</a> of it:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; from bs4 import BeautifulSoup
&gt;&gt;&gt; 
&gt;&gt;&gt; html_content = """
... &lt;body&gt;
...     &lt;p&gt;test&lt;/p&gt;
... &lt;/body&gt;
...  """
&gt;&gt;&gt; soup = BeautifulSoup(html_content, "lxml")
&gt;&gt;&gt; p = soup.p
&gt;&gt;&gt; print(p.name)
p
</code></pre>
<p>With <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/html.parser.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>html.parser</code></a> though you can listen to "start" and "end" tag "events".</p>
</div>
