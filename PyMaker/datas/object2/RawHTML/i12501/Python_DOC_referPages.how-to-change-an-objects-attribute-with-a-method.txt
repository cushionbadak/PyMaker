<p>I have this code that stores a person's name, age and funds. I am trying to write a method that can take "age" or "funds", along with a number, and increase the given attribute by that number.</p>
<pre><code>class Actor:
    def __init__(self, name, age, funds):
        self.name
        self.age = age
        self.funds = funds

    def increase_age(self, increase_amount=1):
        self.age = self.age + increase_amount

    def increase_attrib(self, attrib, increase_amount=1):
        self.attrib = self.attrib + increase_amount

a = Actor("Andrea", 32, 10000)
</code></pre>
<p><code>a.increase_age()</code> works fine: calling it increases the age of Andrea to 33, just as expected. However, <code>a.increase_attrib("age")</code> gives an error, saying <code>AttributeError: 'Actor' object has no attribute 'attrib'</code>. <code>a.increase_attrib("funds")</code> gives similar results.</p>
<p>If I just say <code>a.increase_attrib(age)</code> (without the quotes) I get a NameError, which is what I expected.</p>
<p>By my understanding, giving the parameter <code>"age"</code> to <code>increase_attrib()</code> should mean that the <code>attrib</code> mentioned becomes <code>age</code>, so that <code>increase_attrib()</code> references <code>self.age</code> instead of <code>self.attrib</code>. Apparently, I was wrong.</p>
<p>Now, I could just use <code>increase_age()</code> instead, but then I'd have to make different methods for age and funds, and even more methods once I add other features to <code>Actor</code>, like location, gender, and nationality.</p>
<p>What do I need to do so that I can pass the name of an attribute to a method and have it change that attribute?</p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You are looking for <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/functions.html#setattr" rel="nofollow"><code>setattr</code></a>:</p>
<pre><code>setattr(obj, 'foo', 42)
</code></pre>
<p>Is the same as</p>
<pre><code>obj.foo = 42
</code></pre>
<p>So for your example:</p>
<pre><code>def increase_attrib(self, attrib, increase_amount=1):
    setattr(self, attrib, getattr(self, attrib, 0) + increase_amount)
</code></pre>
</div>
