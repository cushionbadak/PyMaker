<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You can't access <code>self</code> without explicitly pass it. (using method or lambda)</p>
<p>More preferably, define <code>progress_hook</code> as instance method:</p>
<pre><code>def progress_hook(self, txt):
    self.txtProg.setText(txt)
</code></pre>
<p>and bind it as follow:</p>
<pre><code>def launchit(self, MainWindow):
    MainWindow.setWindowTitle('Launching..')
    with TheDLClass() as dl:
        dl.fd.add_progress_hook(self.progress_hook) # &lt;----
        dl.download(['http://myurl.com/'])
</code></pre>
