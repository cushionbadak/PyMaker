<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>I suggest you simply use a global variable. If you want to share the data of the variable thevar for example:
On the very top of your script:</p>
<pre><code>thevar = 0
global thevar
</code></pre>
<p>And in every thread where thevar is used:</p>
<pre><code>global thevar
</code></pre>
<p>That's it, now all threads can access thevar and store their computations there.</p>
