<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You need to add another pair of parentheses around <code>yield ...</code>:</p>
<pre><code>def well_known_generator():
    print('start')
    print((yield 100))
    print((yield 200))
    print((yield 300))
    print('end')
</code></pre>
<p>The parentheses are <em>part of the <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/reference/expressions.html#yield-expressions" rel="noreferrer"><code>yield</code> expression syntax</a></em>:</p>
<pre><code>yield_atom       ::=  "(" yield_expression ")"
</code></pre>
<p>but the parentheses are optional when it's the only expression in an assignment statement or a statement expression:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>The parentheses may be omitted when the yield expression is the sole expression on the right hand side of an assignment statement.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Inside a call expression (such as <code>print(...)</code>), the parentheses can't be omitted.</p>
