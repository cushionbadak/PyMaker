<p>Try the following, and tinker with the values.</p>
<pre><code>import turtle

turtle.setup(1000,1000)
turtle.hideturtle()

turtle.speed(0)
turtle.up()
turtle.goto(0,0)
turtle.down()
n = 20
for i in range(n):
    turtle.up()
    turtle.backward(150)
    turtle.right(15)
    turtle.down()

    turtle.circle(100)

    turtle.up()
    turtle.left(15)
    turtle.forward(150)
    turtle.up()

    turtle.left(360/n)
</code></pre>
