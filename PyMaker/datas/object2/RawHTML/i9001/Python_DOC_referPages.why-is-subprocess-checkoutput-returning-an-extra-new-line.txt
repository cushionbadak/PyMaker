<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>By default <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/functions.html#print" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>print</code></a> always adds a newline. </p>
<blockquote>
<p>print(*objects, sep=' ', <strong>end='\n'</strong>, file=sys.stdout, flush=False)</p>
</blockquote>
<p>This can be supressed:</p>
<pre><code>print(output, end='')
</code></pre>
</div>
