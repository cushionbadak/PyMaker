<p>Use <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/re.html#re.findall" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><strong><em>re.findall()</em></strong></a> function:</p>
<pre><code>import re 

with open('lines.txt', 'r') as fh:
    contents = fh.read()
    negs = re.findall(r"'neg':\s?(\d+\.\d+)\}", contents, re.M)
</code></pre>
<p>Additional approach to get <em>the words which have negative value</em>:</p>
<pre><code>neg_words = re.findall(r"^([-\w.]+)\s?\{[^{]+?(?='neg':\s?-\d+\.\d+\})", contents, re.M)
</code></pre>
