<p>I'm trying to get a hash of a string:</p>
<pre><code>        hs = hashlib.sha256(get_some_string()).hexdigest()
</code></pre>
<p>An error:</p>
<pre><code>TypeError: Unicode-objects must be encoded before hashing
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Use <code>utf-8</code> encoding:</p>
<pre><code>hs = hashlib.sha256(get_some_string().encode('utf-8')).hexdigest()
</code></pre>
<p>To get more information read <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/howto/unicode.html#converting-to-bytes" rel="nofollow noreferrer">documentation</a>.</p>
