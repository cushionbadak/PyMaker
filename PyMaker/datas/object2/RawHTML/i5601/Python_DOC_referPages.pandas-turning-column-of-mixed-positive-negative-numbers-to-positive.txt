<p>In my dataframe I have a column containing numbers, some positive, some negative. Example</p>
<pre><code>    Amount
0  -500
1   659
3   -10
4   344
</code></pre>
<p>I want to turn all numbers Df['Amount'] into positive numbers. I thought about multiplying all numbers with *-1. But though this turns negative numbers positive, and also does the reverse. </p>
<p>Is there a better way to do this?</p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You can assign the result back to the original column:</p>
<pre><code>df['Amount'] = df['Amount'].abs()
</code></pre>
<p>Or you can create a new column, instead:</p>
<pre><code>df['AbsAmount'] = df['Amount'].abs()
</code></pre>
