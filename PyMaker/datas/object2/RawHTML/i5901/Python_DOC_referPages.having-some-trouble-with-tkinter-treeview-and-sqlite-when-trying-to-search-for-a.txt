<p>Read doc <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/tkinter.ttk.html#tkinter.ttk.Treeview.insert" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Treeview.insert</a>. </p>
<p><code>insert()</code> has parameter <code>index</code> to choose row. Or you can use word <code>end</code> to put after last row.</p>
<hr/>
<p><code>delete()</code> needs <code>id</code> of inserted row/item (not row number)</p>
<pre><code>item_id = treeview.insert(...)
</code></pre>
<p>and later</p>
<pre><code>treeeview.delete(item_id)
</code></pre>
