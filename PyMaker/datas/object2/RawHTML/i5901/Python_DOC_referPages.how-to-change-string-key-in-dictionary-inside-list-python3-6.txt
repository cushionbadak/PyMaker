<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Just loop over the data and change the dicts appropriately assigning to the 
<code>'sender'</code> key what you <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/stdtypes.html#dict.pop" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>pop</code></a> from <code>'user_id'</code>:</p>
<pre><code>for d in data:
    d['sender'] = d.pop('user_id')
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p><strong>What about without loop ?</strong></p>
<p>One line Solution:</p>
<pre><code>list(map(lambda x:x.__setitem__('sender',x.pop('user_id')),your_data))
</code></pre>
