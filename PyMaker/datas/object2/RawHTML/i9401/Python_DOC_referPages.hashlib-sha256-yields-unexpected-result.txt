<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>you want to <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/binascii.html#binascii.unhexlify" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>unhexlify</code></a> your hexadecimal representation:</p>
<pre><code>from binascii import unhexlify
from hashlib import sha256
master_key = unhexlify("02F840A04114081690223B7069071A70D6DABB891763B638CC20C7EC3BD58E6C86")
print(sha256(master_key).hexdigest())
# cb05d0fd5e76ba8ea88323fc5d3eefd09a78d8e2a5fd4955307b549657a31330
</code></pre>
</div>
