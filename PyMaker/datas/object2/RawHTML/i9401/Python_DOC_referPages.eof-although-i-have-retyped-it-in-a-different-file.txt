<p>You can't have a <code>try</code> clause without an accompanying <code>except</code>; that's the cause of your error.</p>
<p>See the docs on <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/tutorial/errors.html#handling-exceptions" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Handling Exceptions</a>.</p>
