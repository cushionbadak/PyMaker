<p>From what I understand, you can just <em>split on 2 or more spaces</em> with <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/re.html#re.split" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>re.split()</code></a>:</p>
<pre><code>In [1]: import re

In [2]: met = 'KIRKENES (CIV/MIL)              -8.666667       5.350000       5.866667      HORNSUND RIVER     ENAN      7.9999
    ...: 998E-02  93  85  2.0000000E-02  0.1600000      4.9999997E-02      -999.9000      -999.9000      8  7  3  22.50000'

In [3]: re.split(r"\s{2,}", met)
Out[3]: 
['KIRKENES (CIV/MIL)',
