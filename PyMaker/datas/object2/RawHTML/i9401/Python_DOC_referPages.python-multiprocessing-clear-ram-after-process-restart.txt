<p>Sometimes I need to restart the processes for changing some settings.</p>
<p>Is there a possibility to clear the data, which are saved in the RAM. I saw my RAM usage is growing ~2% / restart.</p>

<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You can't explicitly free memory. What you can do is call Garbage Collector with <code>gc.collect()</code> according to <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/gc.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Python Documentation</a>.</p>
