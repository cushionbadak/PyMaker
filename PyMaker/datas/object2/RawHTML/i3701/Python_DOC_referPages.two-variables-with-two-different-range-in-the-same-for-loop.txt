<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>The answer is to use <code>zip</code>:</p>
<pre><code>for letters_num ,a ,z in zip(range(0,26), range(1,27), range(26,0,-1)):
    print 'letter' ,letters[letters_num] ,'is number' ,a,'from a and number',z,'from z' 
</code></pre>
