<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>It does not have a specific name and is just listed under string sequence operations in official document: </p>
<p><a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/stdtypes.html#common-sequence-operations" rel="nofollow noreferrer">https://docs.python.org/3/library/stdtypes.html#common-sequence-operations</a></p>
<p>In some online courses this is simply referred as <strong>String Repetition</strong>. </p>
