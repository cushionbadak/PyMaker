<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Since you asked two very similar questions in quick succession of each other, I will simply modify my answer from the first question to answer this question.</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; my_source = r'AB\CDEFG_HI(JKLM).NOP'
&gt;&gt;&gt; deletions = ('_', r'\', '(', ')', 'EF', 'O')
&gt;&gt;&gt; for deletion in deletions:
...     my_source = my_source.replace(deletion, "")
... 
&gt;&gt;&gt; my_source
'ABCDGHIJKLM.NP'
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Why use regex for such a simple task. Use <a href="http://docs.python.org/3/library/string.html#string.replace" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>str.replace()</code></a>:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; s = "Hello \\ world \ !"
&gt;&gt;&gt; s.replace("\\", "")
'Hello  world  !'
</code></pre>
