<pre><code>ðŸ’„ -&gt; 128132 -&gt; Lipstick

<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You can use the <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/unicodedata.html#unicodedata.name" rel="nofollow"><code>unicodedata.name()</code> function</a> to look up the official codepoint name:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; import unicodedata
&gt;&gt;&gt; unicodedata.name(chr(128132))
'LIPSTICK'
&gt;&gt;&gt; unicodedata.name(chr(128133))
'NAIL POLISH'
&gt;&gt;&gt; unicodedata.name(chr(128139))
'KISS MARK'
</code></pre>
