<p><a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/pdb.html" rel="nofollow"><code>pdb</code></a> is just a normal module thatâ€™s shipped as part of the standard library. As such, you can just look into your local <code>lib</code> folder to find the <code>pdb.py</code> source.</p>
<p>Of course, you can also look at it online, e.g. on the <a href="https://hg.python.org/cpython/file/3.4/Lib/pdb.py" rel="nofollow">Python source repository</a>.</p>
