<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>In <code>randint()</code> You should specify two integers:beginning and end. The first value should be less than the second.
In your case:</p>
<pre><code>print(random.randint(0,5))
</code></pre>
<p>This will output either 0, 1, 2, 3, 4 or 5. </p>
