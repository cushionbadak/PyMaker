<p>I'm trying to create a new column, a tuple based on two existing columns:</p>
<pre><code>dff['net_sys']= dff[['network', 'syscode']].apply(lambda x: (x['network'], x['syscode']),axis=1)
</code></pre>
<p>I'm getting an error:  </p>
<pre><code>TypeError: list indices must be integers or slices, not list
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Using </p>
<pre><code>dff['net_sys']= dff[['network', 'syscode']].apply(tuple,axis=1)
</code></pre>
<p>Or </p>
<pre><code>dff['net_sys']=list(zip(dff['network'],dff['syscode']))
</code></pre>
