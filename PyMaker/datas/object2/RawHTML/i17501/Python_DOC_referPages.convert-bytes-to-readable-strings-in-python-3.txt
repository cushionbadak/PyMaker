<p>Use <a href="http://docs.python.org/3/library/binascii.html#binascii.hexlify" rel="nofollow"><code>binascii.hexlify</code></a>:</p>

b'0074df6540'
</code></pre>

<span class="comment-copy">By "characters I can read", do you mean something you can look at and understand, or do you just want characters? Printing it already meets the requirement, strictly speaking; you get characters <code>b</code>, <code>'</code>, <code>x</code>, <code>0</code>, etc., and you can read them. On the other hand, if you want "readable" in the sense of something you can understand, you may be out of luck. For all you know, this data is a JPG or a RAR archive.</span>
