<p>Because that syntax is invalid.</p>
<p>Iterable unpacking is allowed in various circumstances – for example:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://docs.python.org/3/tutorial/controlflow.html#unpacking-argument-lists" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Unpacking Argument Lists</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.python.org/dev/peps/pep-3132/" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Assignment</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.python.org/dev/peps/pep-0448/" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Comprehensions</a></li>
</ul>
<p>… but not universally, and definitely not for indexing. In fact, it's unclear what your proposed syntax even means. Consider: what would <code>a[*b]</code> return if <code>b</code> were <code>[1, 2]</code>?</p>
