<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Consider the following code:</p>
<pre><code>from  enum import Enum

class SubclassOfEnum(Enum):
    x = 5
print(SubclassOfEnum.x)

class SubSubclassOfEnum(SubclassOfEnum):
    y = 6
print(SubSubclassOfEnum.y)
</code></pre>
<p>We get an error, <code>TypeError: Cannot extend enumerations</code>,</p>
<p>from: <code>Python36\lib\enum.py", line 436, in _get_mixins_</code></p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Because subclassing <code>Enum</code>s with members is <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/enum.html#restricted-subclassing-of-enumerations" rel="nofollow noreferrer">specifically disallowed</a>.</p>
