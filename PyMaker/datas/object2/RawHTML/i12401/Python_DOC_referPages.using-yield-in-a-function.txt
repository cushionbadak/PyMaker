<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Your error is here:</p>
<pre><code>for i in n:
</code></pre>
<p><code>n</code> is an integer and not an iterable. Perhaps you wanted to use <a href="https://docs.python.org/2/library/functions.html#xrange" rel="nofollow"><code>xrange()</code></a> (Python 2 only) or <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/stdtypes.html#typesseq-range" rel="nofollow"><code>range()</code></a> (recommended on Python 3) here:</p>
<pre><code>for i in range(n):
</code></pre>
<p>Note that this starts iteration at <em>0</em>, not 1 (up to and including <code>n - 1</code>). You could either use <code>range(1, n + 1)</code>, or simply add 1 to your sum:</p>
<pre><code>def suite(n):
    total = 0
    for i in range(n):
        total += i + 1
        yield total
</code></pre>
<p>This hasn't really got anything to do with generators; wether or not you used <code>yield</code>, trying to loop over a plain <code>int</code> object doesn't work either way.</p>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Because your function declaration doesn not correspond with for loop. You cannot iterate over integer, you should use some iterable instead. The simplest way is to use range:</p>
<p>The correct version is:</p>
<pre><code>def suite(n):
    total = 0
    for i in range(n):
        total += i
        yield total
&gt;&gt;&gt;suite(6)
</code></pre>
<p>Or, you can do another change to yield sum of some iterable:</p>
<pre><code>def suite(iterable):
    total = 0
    for i in iterable:
        total += i
        yield total

&gt;&gt;&gt;suite([1,2,3])
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Use <a href="https://docs.python.org/2/library/functions.html#range" rel="nofollow">range</a> to create an iterator to use in your <code>for</code> loop:</p>
<pre><code>def suite(n):
    total = 0
    for i in range(1, n+1):
        total+=i
        yield total
</code></pre>
