<p>Try this:</p>
<pre><code>import re

input_ = 'fafaf fadfa fafa fa-fa faf_afa@gs!' 
input_ = re.sub('[^0-9a-zA-Z]+', '', input_)
</code></pre>

<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>If you want another option:</p>
<pre><code>s='fafaf fadfa fafa fa-fa faf_afa@gs!'
s.translate(str.maketrans('', '','_-@ !'))
</code></pre>
<p>Output:</p>
<pre><code>'fafaffadfafafafafafafafags'
</code></pre>
<p>The third argument of str.maketrans(), it must be a string, whose characters will be mapped to None in the result. That means they will be deleted.</p>
<p>And the documents: <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/stdtypes.html?highlight=maketrans#str.maketrans" rel="nofollow noreferrer">str.maketrans()</a> and <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/stdtypes.html?highlight=maketrans#str.translate" rel="nofollow noreferrer">str.translate()</a></p>
