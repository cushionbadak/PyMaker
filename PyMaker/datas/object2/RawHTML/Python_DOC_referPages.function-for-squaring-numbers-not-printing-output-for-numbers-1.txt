<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Your loop part is iterating from <code>1</code> to the <code>input_integer+1</code>. Thus if you give 0 or any negative integer, <code>range(1, number+1)</code> turns into a vacant iterable, thus it doesn't print anything.</p>
<p>Try this :</p>
<pre><code>def print_squares_to_number(number):
    """takes number and prints a table of all integers and their squares from one to number"""
    if number &lt; 1:
        print("ERROR: number must be at least 1")
    else:
        for numbers in range(1, number + 1):
            print(numbers, "*", numbers, "=", numbers*numbers)

print_squares_to_number(0)
print_squares_to_number(5)
print_squares_to_number(-1)
</code></pre>
<p><strong>OUTPUT</strong> :</p>
<pre><code>ERROR: number must be at least 1
1 * 1 = 1
2 * 2 = 4
3 * 3 = 9
4 * 4 = 16
5 * 5 = 25
ERROR: number must be at least 1
</code></pre>
