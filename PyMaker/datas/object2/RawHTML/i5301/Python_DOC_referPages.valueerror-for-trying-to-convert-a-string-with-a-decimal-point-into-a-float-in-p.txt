<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>If you have a input with space separated numbers, you can split them in parts with <code>split</code>:</p>
<pre><code>ratio = input('ratio numbers: ').split()

r1 = float(ratio[0]) * float(ratio[1])
r2 = float(ratio[2]) * float(ratio[3])
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p><code>list(input(...))</code> makes a list of <em>each individual character</em> in the input, which is probably not what you wanted.</p>
<p>Try this instead:</p>
<pre><code>ratio = input('ratio numbers: ').split()
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Use python's <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/stdtypes.html#str.split" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>split()</code></a> function which splits, by default, on all whitespace characters and will give you <code>list</code> of numbers.</p>
<pre><code>ratio = input('ratio numbers: ').split()
</code></pre>
<p>Then, your <code>float</code> conversion &amp; multiplication would work.</p>
