<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You were close, it's just:</p>
<pre><code>&gt;&gt;&gt; [str(d) for d in S_acc]
['14674.04881484484319092894299', '1287243.594400802980099508539']
</code></pre>
<p>Use <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/string.html#formatspec" rel="nofollow noreferrer">format specs</a> if you want more control over the representation.  </p>
</div>
<span class="comment-copy">You cannot have them as floats, you lose accuracy that way.</span>
