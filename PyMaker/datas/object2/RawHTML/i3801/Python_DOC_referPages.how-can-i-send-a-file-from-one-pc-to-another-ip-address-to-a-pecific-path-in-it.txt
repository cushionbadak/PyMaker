<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>File transfer, at least if it's not the store-on-floppy-and-carry-to-other-computer kind, <strong><em>by definition</em></strong> requires a listening component (/process) on the receiving computer and a sending process on the transmitting computer.  That is client-server <strong><em>by definition</em></strong>.  So your saying "I don't need full client-server" most of all suggests that you have hardly any clue of what you're asking for.</p>
<p>You're clearly asking "how can I get this simple job done in two or at most three simple commands" and that's just not how it works.</p>
</div>
