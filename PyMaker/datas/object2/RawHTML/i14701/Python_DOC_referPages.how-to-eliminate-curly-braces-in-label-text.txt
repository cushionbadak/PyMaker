<p>How to eliminate curly braces around "There are total" and "entries in dictionary"</p>
<p><img alt="enter image description here" src="https://i.stack.imgur.com/sXneJ.png"/></p>
<pre><code>self.status_bar = Label(self.status_bar, text=("There are total", len(dictionary_data), "entries in dictionary."), bg="yellow", relief=FLAT)
</code></pre>
</div>
<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>The problem is that you are currently passing a tuple for the <code>text</code> parameter.  Instead, you should be passing a string.  You can use <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/stdtypes.html#str.format" rel="nofollow"><code>str.format</code></a> to insert the dictionary length into this string:</p>
<pre><code>self.status_bar = Label(self.status_bar, text="There are total {} entries in dictionary.".format(len(dictionary_data)), bg="yellow", relief=FLAT)
</code></pre>
</div>
