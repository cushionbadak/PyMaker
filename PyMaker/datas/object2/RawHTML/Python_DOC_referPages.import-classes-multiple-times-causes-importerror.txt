<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You have created circular imports: adviser imports Episode from episode, which in turn imports Adviser from adviser.</p>
<p>See more here:</p>
<p><a href="https://stackoverflow.com/questions/744373/circular-or-cyclic-imports-in-python">Circular (or cyclic) imports in Python</a></p>
