<p>Maybe you can try to use <strong>zip</strong> keyword</p>
<pre><code>a = {'life':'1','arts':'2'}
b = {'technology':'3', 'culture':'4'}
for q, s in zip(a, b):
    print(q, a[q])
    print(s, b[s])
</code></pre>
