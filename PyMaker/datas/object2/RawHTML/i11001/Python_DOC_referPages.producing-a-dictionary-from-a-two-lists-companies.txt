<p>A simple way would be to use <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/collections.html#collections.defaultdict" rel="nofollow">collections.defaultdict</a>:</p>
<pre><code>answer = defaultdict(set)
for agency, company in zip(agencies, companies):
    answer[agency].add(company)
</code></pre>
