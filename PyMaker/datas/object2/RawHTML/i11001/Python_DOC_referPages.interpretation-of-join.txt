<div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Since you're doing print in a for loop, 
everytime the loop is iterating through the row, 
it's doing a new print statement on the next line. </p>
<p>Think of it this way.</p>
<pre><code>for i in range(5):
    print (i)
</code></pre>
<p>makes </p>
<pre><code>0
1
2
3
4
</code></pre>
