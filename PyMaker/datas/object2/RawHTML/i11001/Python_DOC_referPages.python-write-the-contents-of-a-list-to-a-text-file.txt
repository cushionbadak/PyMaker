<p>Your problem is in this line:</p>
<p><code>for i in user_list:
     i.write(user_list, '\n')</code></p>
<p>Change it to this:</p>
<pre><code>for i in user_list:
    wr.write(i, '\n')
</code></pre>
<p><strong>EDIT:</strong> And remove <code>user_list</code> parameter from all of your functions because it is a global variable and you can access it from within your any function. This may also be a reason why your list not getting saved.</p>
