You want two or three characters at the start of the string followed by a dot and then maybe a space. As a regular expression this looks like ^\w{2,3}\. ?.Now you can use re.sub to replace this part with an empty string.cleaned_name = re.sub(r'(^\w{2,3}\. ?)', r'', name)
